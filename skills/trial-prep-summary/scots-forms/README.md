# Scotland/UK Forms Reference, Proof Preparation Summary

## Key UK Forms (in lieu of US trial documents)

| US Form / Document | Scottish Equivalent |
|---|---|
| Complaint | Initial Writ (Court of Session) / Summons (Sheriff Court) |
| Answer | Defences / Notice of Intention to Defend |
| Counterclaim | Counterclaim (same term in Sheriff Court) |
| Deposition transcript | Precognition (written proof of evidence, confidential) |
| Discovery requests | Specification of Documents; Commission and Diligence |
| Interrogatories | Written questions (rare, not standard practice) |
| Exhibit list | Inventory of Productions |
| Exhibit | Production |
| Motion in limine | Preliminary pleas-in-law / procedural motion |
| Jury instructions | Judge's charge (Court of Session jury trial) |
| Trial brief | Note of Argument / Written Submissions |
| Verdict | Verdict (same term; differing procedures) |
| Judgment | Decree / Interlocutor |
| Bill of costs | Account of Expenses |
| Summary judgment motion | Motion for summary decree |

## Key UK Legislation (Scotland-specific)

- Courts Reform (Scotland) Act 2014
- Civil Litigation (Expenses and Group Proceedings) (Scotland) Act 2018
- Prescription and Limitation (Scotland) Act 1973
- Civil Evidence (Scotland) Act 1988
- Law Reform (Miscellaneous Provisions) (Scotland) Act 1968 (hearsay in civil proceedings)
- Administration of Justice (Scotland) Act 1972 (s.1 - recovery of documents)

## Key Court Rules

- **Sheriff Court Ordinary Cause Rules 1993** (SSI 1993/1956)
- **Sheriff Court Simple Procedure Rules 2016** (SSI 2016/250)
- **Court of Session Rules 1994** (SI 1994/1443)
- **Chapter 43** - Commercial Actions (Court of Session)
- **Practice Note No. 1 of 2020** - Electronic Disclosure (Commercial Actions)

## Key Practitioner References

- **Macphail's Sheriff Court Practice** (4th ed.) - standard Scottish civil procedure text
- **The Laws of Scotland: Stair Memorial Encyclopaedia** - authoritative Scottish law reference
- **Judicial College Guidelines for Personal Injury in Scotland** - PI solatium/valuation guidelines
- **Green's Litigation Styles** - Scottish court styles and precedents
- **Parliament House Book** - Court of Session practice manual

## Relevant Organisations

- **Scottish Courts and Tribunals Service (SCTS)** - court administration
- **Law Society of Scotland** - solicitors' regulatory body
- **Faculty of Advocates** - advocates' regulatory body
- **Sheriff Appeal Court** - appeals from Sheriff Court
- **Auditor of Court** - taxation of judicial expenses
