# Chapter 56A - Judgements Of The Supreme Court

CHAPTER 56A

                          JUDGEMENTS OF THE SUPREME COURT

Applications to apply judgments of the Supreme Court

56A.1.—(1) An application to apply a judgment of the Supreme Court in a cause shall be made by
motion in the Single Bills.

         (2) On enrolling a motion under paragraph (1), a party shall lodge four copies of the Supreme
Court judgment in process.
