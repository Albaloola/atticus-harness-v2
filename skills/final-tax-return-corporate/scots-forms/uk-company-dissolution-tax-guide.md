# UK Company Dissolution, Corporation Tax Checklist

## Key Legislation
- **Corporation Tax Act 2010 (CTA 2010)** - loss relief (ss.37-45), terminal loss relief (s.45)
- **Taxation of Chargeable Gains Act 1992 (TCGA 1992)** - s.122 (capital distributions), s.17 (market value)
- **Capital Allowances Act 2001 (CAA 2001)** - balancing allowances/charges
- **Companies Act 2006** - s.830 (distributable profits), ss.1000-1003 (striking-off)
- **Insolvency Act 1986** - winding up procedures
- **Insolvency (Scotland) Rules 2016** - Scottish winding-up procedure

## HMRC Key Contacts
- **Corporation Tax Online** - https://www.gov.uk/guidance/corporation-tax-online
- **HMRC Company Helpline** - 0300 200 3410
- **HMRC Clearance** - formal clearance letter strongly recommended

## Filing Timeline
| Event | Timing |
|---|---|
| Final CT600 due | 12 months after end of final accounting period |
| Payment of tax | 9 months + 1 day after period end |
| VAT deregistration | Within 30 days of ceasing to trade / dissolution |
| PAYE scheme closure | Same time as final RTI/P35 submission |
| Companies House dissolution | After tax clearance obtained |

## Distribution Tax Treatment (Scotland/UK)
1. **Members' Voluntary Liquidation (solvent)**:
   - Liquidator distributes surplus to shareholders
   - Each distribution is a part-disposal of shares (TCGA 1992 s.122)
   - Shareholder computes capital gain/loss on deemed disposal
   - Only if company has distributable profits: some may be dividends

2. **Creditors' Voluntary Liquidation (insolvent)**:
   - Rarely any shareholder distributions
   - Creditors paid in statutory order (prescribed part, floating charge holders, unsecured creditors)

## Scotland-Specific Points, Insolvency (Scotland) Rules 2016 govern MVL/CVL process, Registered office in Scotland → winding-up petitions to Sheriff Court / Court of Session, Accounts filed at Companies House Edinburgh, HMRC compliance same for Scottish and English/Welsh companies
