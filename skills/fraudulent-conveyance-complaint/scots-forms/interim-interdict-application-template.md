# Interim Interdict Application, Scotland Template

## Orders sought

Interim interdict preventing [act] pending determination of [claim/application].

## Test and submissions

Address prima facie case, balance of convenience, urgency, preservation of status quo, undertaking/caution if required, service, and why damages are inadequate.

## Evidence

Attach affidavit/statement, productions, chronology, asset evidence, correspondence, and draft interlocutor.
