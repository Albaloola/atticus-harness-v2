Scottish/UK forms and resources for Jury Trial skill (adapted from US FRCP jury demand).

Key resources:
- Rules of the Court of Session 1994, Chapter 37 - Jury Trials, Criminal Procedure (Scotland) Act 1995 - solemn procedure, Courts Reform (Scotland) Act 2014 - ASPIC jury provisions, SCTS Juror information: www.scotcourts.gov.uk/coming-to-court/jurors

Note: No Scottish equivalent to FRCP Rule 38 jury demand filing. Civil jury trial is by right for certain actions (personal injury, defamation) in the Court of Session. Motion for judge-alone trial is the procedural step, not motion for jury trial.
