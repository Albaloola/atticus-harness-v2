### UK Corporate Governance Code 2024 (FRC)

The UK CG Code 2024 is the primary governance framework for UK listed companies. It operates on 'comply or explain' basis.

**Audit Committee Provisions (Code 2024):**

| Provision | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Provision 24 | Audit Committee: minimum three independent NEDs (or two for smaller companies); at least one with recent and relevant financial experience; the committee chair must be a NED |
| Provision 25 | Responsibilities: monitor financial reporting integrity, review internal controls/risk management, oversee internal audit, review whistleblowing arrangements |
| Provision 26 | Internal audit: where no internal audit function, assess annually whether one is needed |
| Provision 27 | Whistleblowing: arrangements for confidential reporting must be in place; Audit Committee should review |

**Remuneration Committee Provisions (Code 2024):**

| Provision | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Provision 31 | Remuneration Committee: consists of independent NEDs; design policy aligned to strategy and long-term success |
| Provision 32 | Advisors: engage independent remuneration consultant; assess independence |
| Provision 33 | Design: clear link to strategy; no rewards for failure; malus and clawback provisions |
| Provision 34 | Termination: mitigate obligation to pay; no reward for failure; consider pension contributions |
| Provision 35 | Metrics: transparent; stretching; aligned to purpose and values; multiple performance measures |

### FRC Minimum Standard for Audit Committees (effective 2024)

Additional requirements for FTSE 350 companies (and considered best practice for others):

| Requirement | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Scope** | Additional to UK CG Code requirements; applies to FTSE 350 |
| **Audit tender** | Every 10 years minimum; documented process |
| **Auditor effectiveness** | Annual evaluation against criteria (quality, independence, effectiveness) |
| **Audit quality indicators** | FRC provides indicators, Audit Committee should review |
| **Non-audit services** | FRC Ethical Standard restrictions; policy on permissible services |
| **External audit report** | Committee to review Auditor's Annual Report (ISA 260) |
| **Committee self-assessment** | Annual assessment of own effectiveness |
| **Resourcing** | Committee must have sufficient resource and authority |

### Directors' Remuneration Report Regulations 2008

The Large and Medium-sized Companies and Groups (Accounts and Reports) Regulations 2008 (SI 2008/3013, as amended)

**Structure:**
- **Policy Report** - forward-looking; binding shareholder vote every 3 years (or on change)
- **Implementation Report** - retrospective; annual advisory shareholder vote
- **Single figure table** - total remuneration for each director
- **Percentage change** - CEO pay vs employee remuneration
- **Relative performance** - TSR graph over 5 years
- **Malus and clawback** - must be disclosed

### Scotland-Specific Notes

- **Companies Act 2006 applies equally in Scotland** - no separate Scottish company law regime
- **Scottish-registered companies** follow same UK CG Code / FCA LR requirements if listed
- **Scottish courts (Court of Session, Outer House)** have jurisdiction for certain Companies Act proceedings
- **No separate Scottish listing regime** - LSE Main Market and AIM apply UK-wide
- **Scottish Limited Partnerships (SLPs)** have different transparency requirements under Economic Crime and Corporate Transparency Act 2023
- **Scottish charitable companies** may have additional OSCR (Scottish Charity Regulator) governance requirements
- **Scottish public sector organisations** (NHS boards, local authorities) have separate audit arrangements under Public Finance and Accountability (Scotland) Act 2000 - not covered by this charter framework

### Recommended Charter Structure (UK)

1. **Authority** - Articles of Association, Companies Act 2006, UK CG Code 2024
2. **Membership** - minimum three independent NEDs per committee; financial expertise on Audit; independence per Code Provision 24/32
3. **Meeting frequency** - Audit: minimum 3 per year (quarterly recommended); Remuneration: minimum 2 per year
4. **Executive sessions** - both committees to meet without management annually
5. **Reporting to board** - formal report after each meeting; escalation of material issues
6. **Indemnity / D&O** - cover for committee members in scope
7. **Annual review** - self-assessment of committee effectiveness
