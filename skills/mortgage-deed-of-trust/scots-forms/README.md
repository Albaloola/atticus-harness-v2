Forms for Scottish standard securities:

- Standard Security deed template: available from Registers of Scotland, www.ros.gov.uk, Scottish Standard Clauses and Practitioners Rules for standard securities: Law Society of Scotland, Calling-up Notice (Form A, Schedule 6): Conveyancing and Feudal Reform (Scotland) Act 1970
- Notice of Default (Form B, Schedule 6): Conveyancing and Feudal Reform (Scotland) Act 1970
- LBTT return: Revenue Scotland, www.revenue.scot

These statutory forms are set out in the 1970 Act itself and do not have downloadable equivalents from the Scottish Courts website. The relevant statutes are available at legislation.gov.uk.
