# Specification of Documents, Scottish Personal Injury Template

## Calls

| Call | Document class | Period | Relevance | Haver |
|---:|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Accident report and investigation materials | [dates] | Liability and causation | Defender |
| 2 | Risk assessments / method statements | [dates] | Foreseeability and breach | Defender |
| 3 | CCTV / photographs / site logs | [dates] | Mechanism and condition | Defender/third party |
| 4 | Training / maintenance / inspection records | [dates] | System of work | Defender |
| 5 | Wage/absence records | [dates] | Quantum | Employer |

## Grounds

Each call is necessary and proportionate because it maps to a pleaded issue and cannot reasonably be obtained by informal request.
