# Chapter 73 - Rectification Of Documents

CHAPTER 73

                              RECTIFICATION OF DOCUMENTS

Application of this Chapter

73.1. This Chapter applies to an application under section 8 of the Law Reform (Miscellaneous
Provisions) (Scotland) Act 1985(a)(rectification of defectively expressed documents).

Form of applications

 73.2.-(1) Subject to paragraph (2), an application to which this Chapter applies shall be made by
petition.
          (2) An application to which this Chapter applies may be made-

        (a) in an action to which Chapter 47 (commercial actions) applies, by summons or by a
            conclusion ancillary to other conclusions in the summons or in a counterclaim; or
        (b) in any other action, by a conclusion in a summons or in a counterclaim.

(a) 1985 c.73.
