# Scotland/UK Forms & References for Standard Security / Voluntary Surrender

## Key Statutory Forms (Conveyancing and Feudal Reform (Scotland) Act 1970)

### Standard Security Forms
- **Form A** - Standard Security (Schedule 2 to the 1970 Act) - used for granting a standard security over heritable property
- **Form B** - Standard Security (Companies) (Schedule 2A to the 1970 Act) - used where the debtor is a registered company
- **Form C** - Deed of Restriction (Schedule 3 to the 1970 Act) - restricts the standard security
- **Form D** - Deed of Variation (Schedule 4 to the 1970 Act) - varies terms of the standard security
- **Form E** - Deed of Discharge (Schedule 5 to the 1970 Act) - discharges the standard security

### Calling-Up & Default Notices
- **Calling-Up Notice** (s.19, 1970 Act) - formal demand for repayment; 2-month notice period
- **Notice of Default** (s.21, 1970 Act) - for non-monetary or monetary default remedies
- **Form 1** (Schedule 6 to the 1970 Act) - format for Calling-Up Notice
- **Form 2** (Schedule 7 to the 1970 Act) - format for Notice of Default

### Key Legislative References
- **Conveyancing and Feudal Reform (Scotland) Act 1970** - primary statute for standard securities
- **Home Owner and Debtor Protection (Scotland) Act 2010** - restricts enforcement for residential properties; requires court application (sheriff) for repossession of dwelling houses
- **Mortgage Rights (Scotland) Act 2001** - spouse/partner occupancy rights protections
- **Tenancy of Shops (Scotland) Act 1949** - protections for certain commercial tenants
- **Sheriff Court (Scotland)** - court for repossession applications under the 2010 Act
- **Court of Session (Scotland)** - higher court for commercial/heritable enforcement actions
- **Registers of Scotland** - Land Register of Scotland (registers property titles)
- **Register of Inhibitions** - registers interdicts affecting heritable property dealings
- **Keeper of the Registers** - Registers of Scotland official; maintains the Land Register

### Scottish Deed Forms
- **Disposition** - standard form for transferring heritable property to a new owner
- **Discharge of Standard Security** - formal deed releasing the lender's standard security
- **Deed of Restriction** - limits the standard security to a reduced principal amount
- **Minute of Agreement** - contractual agreement; can record deficiency waiver arrangements
- **Personal Bond** - the underlying debt obligation, secured by the Standard Security

### HMRC / Tax References
- **HMRC Corporate Intangibles and Loan Relationships** - equivalent of IRC § 108 for corporate debt write-offs
- **HMRC Bad Debt Relief (VAT)** - VAT recovery on written-off debts
- **HMRC Property Income Manual (PIM)** - guidance on property repossession and disposal tax treatment
