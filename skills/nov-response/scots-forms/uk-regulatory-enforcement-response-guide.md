# UK/Scottish Regulatory NOV Response, Quick Reference

## Key Regulators & Gateways
| Agency | NOV Type | Appeals Route |
|---|---|---|
| **SEPA** | Notice of Contravention, VMP, FPN, Enforcement Undertaking | Sheriff Court (summary application s.140 EPA 1990) |
| **HSE** | Improvement/Prohibition Notice, FFI invoice | Employment Tribunal / Sheriff Court |
| **Food Standards Scotland** | Improvement Notice, Prohibition Order, RAN | Sheriff Court |
| **FCA** | Warning Notice, Decision Notice | Upper Tribunal (Tax & Chancery) |
| **Ofcom** | Enforcement Notice | Competition Appeal Tribunal |
| **Local Authority EHO** | Improvement/Prohibition Notice, FPN | Sheriff Court / Justice of the Peace Court |
| **COPFS** | Formal Caution, Prosecution (summary/solemn) | Sheriff Appeal Court / High Court of Justiciary |

## Scotland-Specific Legislation
- **Environmental Protection Act 1990** - Part II (waste), Part III (statutory nuisance)
- **Water Environment and Water Services (Scotland) Act 2003**
- **Pollution Prevention and Control (Scotland) Regulations 2012**
- **Health and Safety at Work etc. Act 1974** (GB-wide)
- **Food Safety Act 1990** (with Food Hygiene (Scotland) Regulations 2006)
- **Criminal Procedure (Scotland) Act 1995** - summary/solemn procedure
- **Human Rights Act 1998** - Art.6 right to fair trial
- **Prescription and Limitation (Scotland) Act 1973** - time bars

## Response Template Outline
1. **Acknowledgment** - receipt, case reference, compliance commitment
2. **Factual Response** - date-by-date timeline; exhibit cross-references
3. **Legal Defence** - statutory interpretation; reasonable practicability; due diligence; exemption/variation
4. **Mitigation** - compliance history, prompt action, no harm, voluntary disclosure
5. **Proposed Resolution** - enforcement undertaking, abatement, penalty reduction
6. **Supporting Evidence** - exhibits schedule
7. **Signature** - authorised person, date

## Sample Opening Paragraph
> We write on behalf of [COMPANY NAME] in response to the Notice of Contravention dated [DATE] (ref: [NOV NUMBER]). Our client acknowledges receipt of the Notice and takes this matter extremely seriously. This response sets out the factual context, legal position, and proposed corrective action. Our client remains committed to full regulatory compliance and cooperative engagement with [REGULATOR].
