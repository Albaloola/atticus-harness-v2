# UK NHS Billing & Fraud Compliance, Guidance Note

## [SCOTS: Note] - No UK Equivalent to "Incident-To" Billing

The US concept of "incident-to billing" (NPP services billed under a supervising physician's NPI at the higher physician rate) has **no direct equivalent** in the UK NHS or private healthcare systems.

### How UK Healthcare Reimbursement Differs

| US Medicare | UK NHS |
|-------------|--------|
| Fee-for-service billing per encounter | Block contract / payment by results / capitation |
| Physician NPI billing at higher rate | No individual practitioner billing to NHS |
| NPP incident-to rule | All clinicians have independent scope of practice |
| FCA liability for improper billing | NHS Counter Fraud Authority (NHSCFA) investigates fraud |
| RAC/OIG audits | NHSCFA / local counter fraud teams |

### What This Means for the Skill

The general methodology of billing compliance (eligibility criteria, supervision requirements, documentation, audit programs, training) remains useful as a framework, but **the specific "incident-to billing" topic is replaced** with UK healthcare fraud compliance.

### UK Healthcare Fraud Framework

- **NHS Counter Fraud Authority (NHSCFA)** - strategic lead for NHS fraud
- **Local Counter Fraud Specialists** - in each NHS Trust / Health Board
- **Prescription fraud** - NHS Business Services Authority
- **Dental fraud** - NHS Dental Services
- **Scottish Public Pensions Agency** - NHS pensions fraud

### Key UK Legislation

- Fraud Act 2006 (fraud by false representation)
- Theft Act 1968 / 1978
- Health and Social Care Act 2008
- National Health Service Act 2006 / NHS (Scotland) Act 1978
- Bribery Act 2010 (where corrupt payments involved)
- Proceeds of Crime Act 2002 (confiscation)

### NHS Scotland Specifics

- NHS Scotland operates under devolved governance (separate from NHS England)
- Healthcare Improvement Scotland (regulatory body)
- Counter fraud services managed by NHS National Services Scotland, Scottish Public Services Ombudsman (SPSO) - handles complaints, NHSCFA jurisdiction extends to Scotland (all UK NHS bodies)

### Private Healthcare in Scotland/UK

- Private Medical Insurance (PMI) - regulated by FCA, Independent sector providers regulated by Healthcare Improvement Scotland, Self-pay and insurance billing follows private patient tariffs, No equivalent of incident-to billing or Medicare rate differentials

### Alternative Compliance Focus for UK Users

Adapt the skill's **methodological framework** to address:
1. **NHS Contract Compliance** - GMS/PMS contracts, LES/DES requirements
2. **Private Medical Billing Compliance** - correct coding, overcharging prevention
3. **Prescription Fraud Prevention** - controlled drug monitoring, FP10 compliance
4. **Referral Compliance** - Choose and Phone / Choose and Book
5. **Continuing Healthcare (CHC)** - eligibility and funding compliance
6. **NHS Continuing Care (Scotland)** - Scottish equivalent of CHC
