# Petition for Reduction, Scotland Template

## Orders sought

Reduction of [deed/decision/transaction] dated [date] and consequential orders.

## Grounds

Identify the legal basis: fraud, facility and circumvention, essential error, ultra vires, insolvency challenge, statutory invalidity, breach of natural justice, or other verified ground.

## Narrative

Provide chronology, parties, document challenged, operative defect, prejudice, remedy, and restoration sought.

## Productions

Attach challenged deed/decision, title documents, correspondence, expert reports, affidavits, statutory source log, and draft order.
