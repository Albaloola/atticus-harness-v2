CHAPTER 8B FAILURE OF ACCUSED TO APPEAR

8B.1	Failure of accused to appear: form of warrant


Failure of accused to appear: form of warrant
8B.1	A warrant for the apprehension of an accused under section 102A of the Act of 1995 (failure of accused to appear in solemn proceedings) shall be in Form 8B.1.
