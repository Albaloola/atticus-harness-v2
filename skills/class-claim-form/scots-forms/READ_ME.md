Scottish/UK forms and resources for Group Proceedings Claim Form skill (adapted from US FRCP 23 class action).

Key resources:
- Civil Litigation (Expenses and Group Proceedings) (Scotland) Act 2018
- Act of Sederunt (Group Proceedings Rules)
- Rules of the Court of Session 1994 (as modified)
- SCTS Group Proceedings page: www.scotcourts.gov.uk/courts-and-tribunals/the-supreme-courts/the-court-of-session/group-proceedings/

Critical note: Scottish group proceedings are OPT-IN only, fundamentally different from US opt-out class actions. Only available in the Court of Session.
