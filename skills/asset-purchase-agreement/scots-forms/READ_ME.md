Scottish/UK forms and resources for Asset Purchase Agreement skill.

Key forms:
- Companies House MR01 - register a charge (security interest)
- UK IPO Patent/TM Assignment forms: www.gov.uk/government/organisations/intellectual-property-office, Land Register of Scotland, Disposition template and registration forms, Revenue Scotland LBTT return: www.revenue.scot, HMRC Corporation Tax returns

Key distinctions:
- No IRS Form 8594 equivalent; tax allocation is contractual, TUPE automatically transfers employees (TUPE 2006)
- Scottish heritable property requires formal disposition + Land Register registration, Use "assignation" (Scots law), not "assignment" despite similar meaning, Stamp Duty Land Tax (SDLT) for E&W; Land and Buildings Transaction Tax (LBTT) for Scotland
