# Note of Appeal / Application Concerning Non-Disclosure, Scotland Template

## Decision appealed / challenged

Identify the refusal, date, decision-maker, material withheld, and procedural route.

## Grounds

1. The material is likely to be material to [defence issue].
2. The refusal undermines preparation/fair trial rights because [reason].
3. Any public-interest concern can be managed by redaction, confidentiality restrictions, judicial inspection, or controlled disclosure.

## Orders sought

Disclosure, judicial inspection, reasons for refusal, preservation order, continuation of diet, or such order as the court considers compatible with fairness.
