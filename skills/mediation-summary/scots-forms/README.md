# Scots Legal Forms, Mediation Summary

## Relevant Scottish Mediation & Court Forms

### Mediation Agreements & Templates
- **SIMI (Scottish Mediation) Model Mediation Agreement** - Standard agreement for mediations in Scotland
- **CEDR Model Mediation Agreement** - Commonly used for commercial cross-border mediations
- **Law Society of Scotland Mediation Clauses** - Standard dispute resolution clauses for contracts

### Court Referral Forms
- **Court of Session, Mediation Referral** - Form for voluntary/court-recommended mediation (RCS Chapter 41A)
- **Sheriff Court, Mediation Referral (Pilot Schemes)** - Forms for specific sheriffdom mediation pilots
- **Act of Sederunt (Simple Procedure) - Mediation Direction** - Simple Procedure mediation referral
- **Minute of Withdrawal** - Lodging with court to withdraw action after settlement
- **Consent Decree / Decree by Consent** - Court order recording agreed settlement terms

### Post-Mediation Documents
- **Minute of Settlement (Scots form)** - Formal written settlement agreement (Scots law)
- **Joint Minute** - Parties' agreed minute to court recording settlement
- **Unilateral Declaration** - If only one party wants to record settlement
- **Decree of Dismissal** - Court order dismissing action following settlement

### Relevant Rules
- **Rules of the Court of Session (RCS)** - Chapter 41A (Mediation), Chapter 41B (Arbitration)
- **Act of Sederunt (Sheriff Court Ordinary Cause Rules) 1993** - Chapter 33A (Mediation)
- **Act of Sederunt (Simple Procedure) 2016** - Rules applicable to Simple Procedure mediations
- **Civil Evidence (Scotland) Act 1988** - Admissibility of without prejudice communications

### Mediator Accreditation
- **SIMI Accredited Mediator List** - Scottish Mediation accredited mediators
- **Law Society of Scotland Mediation Panel** - Solicitor-mediators
- **Faculty of Advocates Mediation Panel** - Advocate-mediators
