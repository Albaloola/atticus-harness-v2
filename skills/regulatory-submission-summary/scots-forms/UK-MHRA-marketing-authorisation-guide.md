# UK Regulatory Submissions, MHRA, SEPA, Ofgem, Ofcom Guidance Note

## MHRA (Medicines) - UK Equivalent to FDA

| US FDA Pathway | UK MHRA Equivalent |
|----------------|-------------------|
| NDA (New Drug Application) | Marketing Authorisation Application (MAA) - National Procedure |
| 510(k) (Premarket Notification) | UKCA marking + conformity assessment (medical devices) |
| BLA (Biologics License) | MAA for biologicals; same procedure |
| ANDA (Abbreviated NDA) | Abridged MAA (generic medicines) |
| IND (Investigational New Drug) | Clinical Trial Authorisation (CTA) via MHRA |
| Breakthrough Therapy | Innovative Licensing and Access Pathway (ILAP) |
| REMS (Risk Evaluation) | Risk Management Plan (RMP) |

## UK Submission Types

### Medicines (Human)
- **National MAA**: MHRA assessment - 210-day timeline
- **International Recognition Procedure (IRP)**: 2 to 4 months; relies on reference regulator (RR) approval
- **Decentralised Procedure**: For UK-wide or Great Britain-wide approval
- **Paediatric Investigation Plan (PIP)**: Required for paediatric development

### Medical Devices, UKCA marking (UK equivalent to CE marking)
- Conformity assessment by UK Approved Bodies, MHRA registration requirement, Post-market surveillance obligations

### Environmental (Scotland)
| US Agency | Scotland/UK Equivalent |
|-----------|----------------------|
| EPA (Environmental Protection) | SEPA (Scottish Environment Protection Agency) |
| NPDES Permit | SEPA Water Use Licence / CAR authorisation |
| RCRA (Hazardous Waste) | SEPA Waste Management Licence / EASR Permit |
| EIS (Environmental Impact Statement) | EIA (Environmental Impact Assessment) under EIA Regulations (Scotland) 2011 |
| CAA (Clean Air Act) | SEPA Pollution Prevention and Control (PPC) permit |

### Environmental Authorisations (Scotland) Regulations 2018 (EASR)
- Integrated authorisation framework (permits, registrations, general binding rules)
- Covers: waste management, water discharges, radioactive substances, pollution prevention, Requires: application fee, supporting evidence, public consultation, determination within specified period

## CTD (Common Technical Document) Format
MHRA accepts ICH CTD format (Modules 1 to 5) for MAAs, same as FDA except:
- Module 1 varies (UK-specific administrative information and labelling)
- EU CTD format used historically; UK version applies post-Brexit, Module 3 (Quality) - same requirements, Module 5 (Clinical) - same requirements
