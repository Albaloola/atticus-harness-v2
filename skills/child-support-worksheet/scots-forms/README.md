# Scottish / UK Child Maintenance Forms Reference

## CMS (Child Maintenance Service) Forms

| Form | Purpose | Source |
|------|---------|--------|
| CMS Application Form | Apply for child maintenance through the statutory scheme | [GOV.UK](https://www.gov.uk/child-maintenance-service) |
| CMS Change of Circumstances | Report changes in income, living arrangements, contact | CMS online portal |
| CMS Variation Application | Apply for variation of the basic rate (s.28A Child Support Act 1991) | CMS online / phone |
| CMS Mandatory Reconsideration | Challenge a CMS decision before appeal | CMS online form |
| CMS Appeal to First-tier Tribunal | Appeal CMS decision (Social Security and Child Support Chamber) | HMCTS Form SSCS1 |
| CMS Financial Information Form | Provide income evidence to CMS (P60, payslips, accounts) | Per CMS request |
| Form C(SA)1 | Child support variation application (legacy CSA cases only) | N/A, CSA legacy only |

## Key Legislation References

- **Child Support Act 1991** - Primary UK statute for CMS child maintenance
- **Child Maintenance and Other Payments Act 2008** - Reformed CSA to CMS
- **Child Support (Maintenance Calculations and Special Cases) Regulations 2008** - Calculation rules
- **Family Law (Scotland) Act 1985** - Court-ordered financial provision on divorce/dissolution (ss.1 to 11) including periodical allowance for children
- **Children (Scotland) Act 1995** - Parental responsibilities and rights; underpins child maintenance obligations
- **Family Law (Scotland) Act 2006** - Reformed paternity, parental responsibilities, and rights; s.28 provides for financial provision where civil partnership dissolved
- **Scotland Act 2016** - Devolution of some social security powers (notably the "Scottish Child Payment") but child maintenance remains reserved to UK Parliament
- **Civil Partnership (Scotland) Act 2020** - Same-sex marriage/civil partnership recognition in Scottish family law
