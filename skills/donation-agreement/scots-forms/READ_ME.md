Scottish/UK forms for Donation Agreement skill (adapted from US 501(c)(3) model).

Forms downloaded:
- HMRC_Gift_Aid_Single_Donation_Declaration.pdf, Gift Aid declaration for single donations, HMRC_Gift_Aid_Multiple_Donation_Declaration.pdf, Gift Aid declaration for multiple donations

Key online resources:
- OSCR charity register search: www.oscr.org.uk, HMRC charity tax information: www.gov.uk/topic/business-tax/charities, HMRC Gift Aid claim online service, Scottish Charity Register: www.charityregister.oscr.org.uk

Note: Scottish charities require both OSCR registration AND HMRC charity reference.
