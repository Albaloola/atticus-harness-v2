# Interlocutor, Summary Decree Granted

## Draft Form

**[FORM, DRAFT]**

IN THE COURT OF SESSION

[Case number]

**PARTY NAME** (Pursuer)
v
**PARTY NAME** (Defender)

**[DATE]**

**INTERLOCUTOR**

The Lord Ordinary [name], having heard [counsel/solicitor for the parties], and having considered the motion for summary decree:

1. Finds that the defender has no defence to [the action / article[s] [x] to [y] of the initial writ];

2. Accordingly grants summary decree against the defender for [specify: £[x] / implement / other remedy];

3. Reserves the question of expenses;

4. [If partial: Remits the remaining claims to procedure roll / order for further procedure as to [remaining heads of claim]];

5. Extracts decree [if appropriate].

**[Signature of Lord Ordinary / Clerk of Court]**

---

*Note: An interlocutor is the formal record of the court's decision in Scottish procedure. Extract decree is the enforceable document.*
