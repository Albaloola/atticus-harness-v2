# Chapter 38

ACT OF ADJOURNAL (CRIMINAL PROCEDURE RULES) 1996 SI 1996/513
SCHEDULE 2
CRIMINAL PROCEDURE RULES 1996

PART VII  Miscellaneous procedures
CHAPTER 38 TRANSFER OF RIGHTS OF APPEAL OF DECEASED PERSONS

38	Applications for transfer under section 303A of the Act of 1995

Applications for transfer under section 303A of the Act of 1995
38	Any application to the appropriate Appeal Court under section 303A of the Act of 1995 for an order authorising a person (the "applicant") as executor, or as the case may be by reason of his having a legitimate interest, to institute or continue any appeal which could have been or has been instituted by a deceased person shall be made in Form 38 and shall be accompanied by a copy of the confirmation of the applicant as executor or evidence of his legitimate interest, as the case may be.
