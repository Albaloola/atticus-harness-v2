# Approval Hearing Petition / Note, Scotland Group Proceedings Template

## Orders sought

Approval of settlement, notice plan, distribution scheme, settlement administrator, expenses/admin costs, and timetable for implementation.

## Hearing materials checklist

- Settlement agreement, Group definition order, Notice evidence, Objection schedule and responses, Quantum/economic model, Expenses/admin costs breakdown, Draft approval interlocutor, Source and authority log

## Submissions outline

1. Competency and jurisdiction.
2. Adequacy of representative party and legal representation.
3. Procedural fairness and notice.
4. Merits/risk comparison.
5. Fairness, reasonableness, and adequacy of settlement.
6. Distribution plan and safeguards.
7. Expenses and administration.
8. Draft orders.
