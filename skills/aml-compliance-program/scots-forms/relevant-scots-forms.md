# Scotland/UK Forms & References for AML Compliance Program

## Key UK Regulatory Forms

| Form | Purpose | Filing Route |
|------|---------|-------------|
| SAR (Suspicious Activity Report) | Internal report to MLRO / external to NCA | NCA SAR Portal |
| DAML (Defence Against Money Laundering) Consent Request | Consent to proceed with suspicious transaction | NCA via SAR Portal |
| NCA Consent Moratorium Period | Extended hold period (31 days + 31 day extension) | NCA SAR Portal |
| Regulated Entity Registration | MLR 2017 registration with HMRC / FCA | HMRC MLR Portal or FCA Connect |
| Beneficial Ownership Register | PSC Register (People with Significant Control) | Companies House (PSC register) |
| Trust Registration Service | Register of express trusts with tax consequence | HMRC TRS Portal |
| NSIA Notification | National Security & Investment Act 2021 | BEIS Investment Security Unit |
| Criminal Finances Act 2017 - Unexplained Wealth Order | High-value asset investigations | High Court / Court of Session |

## Key UK SAR / DAML Procedure

- **Suspicious Activity Report (SAR)** - internal to MLRO, then to NCA if MLRO considers it appropriate
- **Defence Against Money Laundering (DAML) consent** - required before proceeding with transaction to avoid POCA s.327-329 liability
- **Authorised Disclosure** - safe harbour under POCA s.338
- **Protected Disclosures** - s.337 POCA (no tipping-off restriction on protected disclosures to NCA)
- **Consent moratorium** - initial 31 working days from NCA receipt; court can extend up to 186 calendar days
- **No CTR equivalent** - UK does not mandate Currency Transaction Reports; use internal large cash transaction logs
- **Joint Money Laundering Steering Group (JMLSG)** - industry guidance (regularly updated)

## Key UK Differences from US AML

| Aspect | US | UK |
|--------|----|----|
| Currency reporting | CTR > $10,000 | No equivalent |
| Beneficial ownership | FinCEN BOI Registry (enrolling) | PSC Register (active since 2016) |
| Suspicious activity | SAR to FinCEN | SAR to NCA (POCA framework) |
| Consent to transact | Not required; file SAR/continue | DAML consent required (POCA) |
| Tipping-off | Broad prohibition | Strict prohibition (POCA s.342) |
| De-risking | Discouraged | Discouraged by FCA guidance |
| Sanctions lists | OFAC SDN List | OFSI Consolidated List + HMT Sanctions List |
| PEP definition | FinCEN guidance | FATF-based per MLR 2017 Sch.2 |
