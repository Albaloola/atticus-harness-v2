# Chapter 56 - Judgments Of The House Of Lords

CHAPTER 56

                           JUDGMENTS OF THE HOUSE OF LORDS

Applications to apply judgments of the House of Lords

56.1.-(1) An application to apply a judgment of the House of Lords in a cause shall be made by
motion in the Single Bills.

         (2) On enrolling a motion under paragraph (1), the party enrolling it shall lodge four copies
of the House of Lords judgment in process.
