# UK/Scottish IP Assignment, Drafting Checklist

## Statutory References
- **Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988 (CDPA)** - s.11 (ownership), s.90(3) (assignment), ss.77-89 (moral rights), ss.91 (future works)
- **Requirements of Writing (Scotland) Act 1995** - formal validity of documents in Scotland
- **Law of Property (Miscellaneous Provisions) Act 1994** - "with full title guarantee" covenant
- **Companies Act 2006 s.44** - execution of documents by companies

## Key UK Forms & Guidance
- **UK IPO Copyright Notice** - https://www.gov.uk/guidance/copyright-ownership
- **HMRC Employment Status** - Check Employment Status for Tax (CEST) tool
- **IR35 Guidance** - https://www.gov.uk/guidance/understanding-off-payroll-working-ir35

## Sample Clause: IP Assignment (Independent Contractor)
> The Creator hereby assigns to the Hiring Party with full title guarantee all Intellectual Property Rights in the Work throughout the world for the full term of such rights (including any extensions and renewals). This assignment is made under section 90(3) of the Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988. To the extent that any Intellectual Property Rights in the Work do not vest automatically by virtue of this assignment, the Creator shall hold them on trust for the Hiring Party.

## Sample Clause: Moral Rights Waiver
> The Creator irrevocably and unconditionally waives all moral rights under sections 77, 80, 84, and 85 of the Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988 in relation to the Work. This waiver is made in accordance with section 87 of that Act.

## Sample Clause: Future Works
> Any Intellectual Property Rights in works created by the Creator for the Hiring Party after the date of this Agreement shall automatically vest in the Hiring Party by way of present assignment of future copyright under section 91 of the Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988.
