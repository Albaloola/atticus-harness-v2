# Group Proceedings Settlement Agreement, Scotland Template

> Working template for Court of Session group proceedings under the Civil Litigation (Expenses and Group Proceedings) (Scotland) Act 2018 and current Court of Session rules. Verify the current rules, form requirements, practice notes, expenses consequences, and any court directions before use.

## 1. Parties and status

This settlement agreement is made between:

- **Representative party:** [name, address, solicitor, role, authority]
- **Defender/respondent:** [name, address, solicitor]
- **Group members:** persons falling within the group definition authorised by the Court of Session on [date/order], listed or described in Schedule 1.

## 2. Recitals

A. The court authorised group proceedings concerning [short issue].  
B. The representative party was authorised by interlocutor dated [date].  
C. The parties have exchanged information sufficient to evaluate liability, causation, quantum, expenses, and distribution.  
D. The parties intend the settlement to be subject to court approval where required.

## 3. Definitions

Define: Claim, Group Member, Settlement Fund, Administration Costs, Eligible Claim, Opt-Out/Opt-In Deadline, Release, Effective Date, Approval Order, Expenses, Distribution Scheme.

## 4. Settlement consideration

The defender shall pay £[amount] into [escrow/client account] within [x] days of the Effective Date. State whether the figure is inclusive or exclusive of expenses, interest, VAT, administration costs, and representative-party costs.

## 5. Distribution scheme

Set out the allocation methodology:

| Category | Eligibility evidence | Weighting | Cap/deduction | Verification method |
|---|---|---:|---:|---|
| [Category A] | [evidence] | [x] | [x] | [method] |
| [Category B] | [evidence] | [x] | [x] | [method] |

Include dispute review, late claims, residual funds, unclaimed sums, tax treatment, confidentiality, and data-protection provisions.

## 6. Notice to group members

Attach the approved notice. State publication method, direct notice method, objection process, opt-out/opt-in process if applicable, accessibility steps, language support, and response deadline.

## 7. Releases

On the Effective Date, each settling group member releases [defined claims] arising from [facts/time period]. Preserve claims that are not intended to be released, including personal injury, fraud, unknown future losses, regulatory complaints, enforcement rights, or non-settling parties where appropriate.

## 8. No admission

Settlement is without admission of liability unless the parties expressly agree otherwise.

## 9. Court approval and conditions precedent

This agreement is conditional upon: approval order, finality/non-appeal period, payment into the settlement account, approved notice, and any other court-directed step.

## 10. Expenses and administration

Specify responsibility for expenses, counsel fees, solicitor fees, settlement administration, audit/accounting, VAT, outlays, and reporting to the court.

## 11. Evidence and source log

Append: pleadings, interlocutors, group definition order, representative authority, quantum model, expert reports, objections, and source verification log.

## 12. Signature blocks

Representative party / Defender / Solicitors / Settlement administrator.
