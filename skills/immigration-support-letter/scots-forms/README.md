# Scotland/UK Forms Reference, Immigration Support Letters

## Key UK Immigration Forms (in lieu of US USCIS forms)

| US Form Equivalent | UK Equivalent |
|---|---|
| USCIS I-130 Petition | Application for entry clearance as a partner (Appendix FM) |
| USCIS I-129 Petition | Skilled Worker Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS) / Tier 2 |
| USCIS I-485 Adjustment | FLR(M) / FLR(FP) - Further Leave to Remain |
| USCIS I-589 Asylum | Application for asylum (ASF) |
| USCIS N-400 Naturalisation | AN form, Application for naturalisation |
| USCIS I-751 Removal of Conditions | FLR(M) - Further Leave to Remain (partner) |

## Relevant UK Legislation

- Immigration Act 1971
- Immigration Rules (HC 395 as amended)
- Nationality, Immigration and Asylum Act 2002
- Borders, Citizenship and Immigration Act 2009
- Immigration Act 2014 and 2016
- Human Rights Act 1998 (ECHR Article 8 engagements)
