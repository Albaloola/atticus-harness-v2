# Scottish / UK Forms Reference for Forbearance Agreement

## Key Security Documents

| Form / Document | Reference | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Standard Security | Form 4, Schedule to the Conveyancing and Feudal Reform (Scotland) Act 1970 | Primary heritable security over land in Scotland |
| Bond and Floating Charge | Companies Act 2006; Insolvency Act 1986 | Company-level security over moveable and heritable assets; requires registration at Companies House and Land Register |
| Deed of Assignation | Scots common law | Security over intangible moveable property (e.g., book debts, IP) |
| Pledge / Pledge Agreement | Scots common law | Possessory security over corporeal moveable property |
| Trust Deed for Creditors | Insolvency Act 1986 s. 258 | Voluntary arrangement alternative to sequestration (Scottish personal insolvency) |

## Enforcement Forms

| Form | Statute | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Calling-Up Notice (Form A) | Schedule 6, C(FRS)A 1970 | Formal demand for payment under a Standard Security; 2-month statutory calling-up period |
| Notice of Default (Form B) | Schedule 6, C(FRS)A 1970 | Notice of default under Standard Security non-monetary obligations; 21-day compliance period |
| Application for Warrant of Sequestration | Act of Sederunt / standard Sheriff Court form | Commencement of personal insolvency process against debtor |
| Administration Order | Insolvency Act 1986 Schedule B1 | UK company insolvency procedure; administrator appointed to manage affairs |
| Petition for Winding Up | Insolvency Act 1988 & Rules | Company liquidation petition to Court of Session or Sheriff Court |

## Registration

| Registry | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Land Register of Scotland (Registers of Scotland) | Registration of Standard Securities over heritable property |
| Companies House | Registration of floating charges and fixed charges (Register of Charges) |
| Register of Inhibitions | Registration of court decrees; restricts debtor's ability to deal with heritable property |
| Register of Insolvencies | Register of sequestrations, trust deeds, and other insolvency events |

## Key Statutes

| Statute | Relevance |
|---|---|
| Conveyancing and Feudal Reform (Scotland) Act 1970 | Standard Security structure, calling-up, default notices |
| Consumer Credit Act 1974 | Regulated consumer credit agreements; s. 87 requires default notice before enforcement; s. 129 time orders |
| Consumer Rights Act 2015 | Unfair terms in consumer contracts |
| Unfair Contract Terms Act 1977 | Limits exclusions of liability; may affect enforceability of broad waivers in non-consumer contracts |
| Consumer Protection from Unfair Trading Regulations 2008 | Prohibits aggressive/unfair commercial practices |
| Insolvency Act 1986 | Administration, receivership, voluntary arrangements |
| Bankruptcy (Scotland) Act 2016 | Sequestration (personal insolvency) in Scotland |
| Companies Act 2006 (Part 25) | Registration of charges |
| Land Registration (Scotland) Act 2012 | Modern land register, advance notices, registration of Standard Securities |
| Financial Services and Markets Act 2000 | FCA and PRA regulatory framework for lending |
| Late Payment of Commercial Debts (Interest) Act 1998 | Interest on late payments in commercial contracts |
