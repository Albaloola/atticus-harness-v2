# Xeugklsu Chapter 25

ACT OF SEDERUNT (SHERIFF COURT ORDINARY CAUSE RULES) 1993 No.1956 (S.223)

SCHEDULE 1

Initiation and progress of causes

CHAPTER 25 MINUTES OF SIST AND TRANSFERENCE

25.1.	Minutes of sist
25.2.	Minutes of transference

Minutes of sist
25.1	Where a party dies or comes under legal incapacity while a cause is depending, any person claiming to represent that party or his estate may apply by minute to be sisted as a party to the cause.

Minutes of transference
25.2.	Where a party dies or comes under legal incapacity while a cause is depending and the provisions of rule 25.1 are not invoked, any other party may apply by minute to have the cause transferred in favour of or against, as the case may be, any person who represents that party or his estate.
