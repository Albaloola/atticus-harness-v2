# Minute of Admissions

## Draft Form

**[FORM, DRAFT]**

IN THE COURT OF SESSION

[Case number]

**PARTY NAME** (Pursuer)
v
**PARTY NAME** (Defender)

**MINUTE OF ADMISSIONS FOR THE PURPOSES OF [SUMMARY DECREE / PROOF]**

The parties jointly agree the following admissions of fact for the purposes of the [pending motion for summary decree / proof]:

**Admissions**

1. [Admission 1 - single discrete fact, with reference to pleading if applicable]
2. [Admission 2]
3. [Admission 3]

**Status**

These admissions are made without prejudice to the parties' positions on any matter not expressly admitted herein. The parties reserve the right to lead evidence on any disputed fact not covered by this minute.

Signed:

________________________
[Solicitor for Pursuer]

________________________
[Solicitor for Defender]

Date: [date]

---

*Note: A Minute of Admissions is a Scottish procedural mechanism for agreeing facts. It serves a similar function to the US Statement of Undisputed Material Facts but is framed within the Scottish pleading tradition.*
