# Scots Legal Forms, Franchise Transfer Agreement

## Relevant Scottish Business & Franchise Forms

### Assignment / Assignation Documents
- **Assignation of Contract (Scots law)** - Formal written assignation of the franchise agreement and related contracts
- **Assignation of Lease** - Landlord consent and assignation (Scots commercial lease rules)
- **Intimation to Debtor** - Notice to debtors on assignation of receivables

### Regulatory / BFA
- **BFA Membership Application** - British Franchise Association membership for franchisors
- **BFA Code of Ethics Compliance Statement** - Voluntary adherence statement
- **BFA Model Franchise Agreement** - BFA-recommended form (if applicable)

### Company / Property Searches
- **Registers of Scotland Search** - Property and charge register (for heritable property/charges)
- **Companies House Search** - Charges register, company records for corporate parties
- **Landlord Consent (Commercial Lease)** - Standard Scottish commercial lease consent form

### HMRC & Tax
- **Capital Gains Summary (SA108)** - HMRC Self Assessment form for capital gains reporting
- **Business Asset Disposal Relief Election** - HMRC form for reduced CGT rate
- **VAT Transfer of Going Concern (TOGC) Declaration** - HMRC guidance notes
- **Stamp Duty (Share Transfers)** - Stock transfer form (if equity sale)

### Court / Tribunal (Dispute Resolution)
- **Court of Session Commercial Action (Initial Writ)** - Chapter 47A RCS
- **Sheriff Court Simple Procedure** - For lower value franchise disputes
- **Arbitration (Scotland) Act 2010** - Model arbitration clause for franchise agreements

### Other
- **Personal Guarantee (Scots law)** - Standard guarantee wording (Scots form)
- **Certificate of Title (Solicitor)** - Confirming title and charges for heritable property
