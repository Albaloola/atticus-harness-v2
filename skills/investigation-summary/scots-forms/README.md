# Scottish/UK Forms, Competition/Regulatory Investigation Summary

## Key Forms & References

| Purpose | Form / Reference | Issuer |
|---------|-----------------|--------|
| Formal information request | Section 26 Notice (Competition Act 1998) | CMA |
| Power to enter premises | Section 28/28A Warrant | CMA (Sheriff Court in Scotland) |
| Statement of Objections | Formal charging document | CMA |
| Leniency application | CMA Leniency Guidance | CMA |
| Merger notice | Merger Notice (MVS) | CMA |
| Market investigation reference | Enterprise Act 2002 | CMA |
| Commitment undertakings | Section 31A Competition Act 1998 | CMA |

## Scotland-Specific

- **CMA Scotland office**: Edinburgh (Queen Elizabeth House)
- **Dawn raids in Scotland**: Require warrant from Sheriff Court (not magistrates)
- **Court of Session**: Appeals from CMA competition decisions (Inner House)
- **Scottish civil procedure**: Competition follow-on damages actions in Court of Session
- **Sheriff Court**: Lower-value competition claims possible
- **Edinburgh Gazette**: Formal notices for Scottish company matters

## Key UK Statutes

| Statute | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Competition Act 1998 | Prohibitions on anti-competitive agreements (Ch.I) and abuse of dominance (Ch.II) |
| Enterprise Act 2002 | Merger control, market investigations, criminal cartel offence |
| Consumer Rights Act 2015 | Private competition damages actions |
| Company Directors Disqualification Act 1986 | Competition disqualification orders |
