# Scottish Affidavit Template

## Draft Form

**[FORM, DRAFT, VERIFY FORMALITIES WITH CURRENT PRACTICE]**

[COURT DETAILS]

[Case number]

**PARTY NAME** (Pursuer)
v
**PARTY NAME** (Defender)

**AFFIDAVIT OF [FULL NAME]**

I, [full name], residing at [full address], [occupation], SOLEMNLY SWEAR / AFFIRM that:

1. I am the [pursuer/defender/witness] in these proceedings. [Or: state capacity/connection]

2. The facts set forth in this affidavit are within my own personal knowledge and are true to the best of my knowledge and belief.

**Personal knowledge facts:**

[Set out discrete, numbered paragraphs containing only facts within the deponent's direct knowledge. No opinions, no conclusions, no hearsay.]

**Production:**

3. There is produced and shown to me [description of document], marked [Production No.], which is a true copy of the [description].

**Statement of truth:**

4. I believe the facts stated in this affidavit are true.

SWORN at [place] on [date]

Before me,
[Name of notary public / justice of the peace / commissioner for oaths]

[Signature of notary/public official]
[Qualification: Notary Public / JP / Commissioner for Oaths]

---

[Signature of deponent]

---

*Conversions: US "Declaration under penalty of perjury" → Scottish "Affidavit sworn/affirmed before competent authority." Do not use US-style "I declare under penalty of perjury" - Scottish affidavits require proper swearing/affirmation before a notary public, JP, or commissioner for oaths.*
