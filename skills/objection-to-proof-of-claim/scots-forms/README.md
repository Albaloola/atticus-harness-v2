# Scots Legal Forms, Objection to Claim (Insolvency/Sequestration)

## Relevant Scottish Insolvency Forms

### Sequestration (Personal) - Claim Forms
- **Oath of Creditor (Form 5.1)** - Insolvency (Scotland) Rules 2016 prescribed form for submitting claim in sequestration
- **Statement of Claim (Form 5.2)** - Alternative form for submitting claim without oath
- **List of Claims (Form 5.3)** - Trustee's published list of admitted and rejected claims
- **Notice of Objection to List of Claims** - Objection to trustee's adjudication
- **Application to the Sheriff (Section 46)** - Challenge to trustee's decision on a claim

### Liquidation (Corporate)
- **Proof of Debt (Form 6.1)** - Insolvency (Scotland) Rules 2016 form for liquidation claims
- **Statement of Affairs (Form 6.2)** - Directors' statement of company affairs
- **Liquidator's List of Claims** - Adjudication of claims in liquidation
- **Application to Court re Claim** - Challenge to liquidator's decision

### Accountant in Bankruptcy (AiB) Forms
- **AiB Claim Form** - Standard AiB form for claiming in sequestration managed by AiB
- **AiB Notification of Adjudication** - AiB notification of claim admission/rejection
- **Appeal to AiB** - Appeal against trustee's decision on a claim
- **Application to Sheriff (Section 49)** - Appeal against trustee's or AiB's decision

### Sheriff Court / Court of Session
- **Initial Writ (Sequestration Objections)** - For raising an action challenging a claim
- **Defences** - Creditor's defence to objection
- **Interlocutor (Court Order)** - Formal court order admitting/rejecting claim
- **Note of Argument** - Written submission for contested hearing

### Insolvency Practitioner / Trustee Forms
- **Notice to Creditor** - Trustee's notice requiring further evidence
- **Notice of Fixed Date** - Setting last date for submitting claims
- **Dividend Proposal** - Proposing distribution based on admitted claims
- **Report on Claims** - Trustee's report to creditors/court on claims adjudication

### Prescription & Evidence
- **Prescription and Limitation (Scotland) Act 1973** - Statutory provisions for checking time bar
- **Requirements of Writing (Scotland) Act 1995** - Formal validity requirements for documents of debt
- **Civil Evidence (Scotland) Act 1988** - Admissibility of documentary evidence

### Key Statutory Instruments
- **Insolvency (Scotland) Rules 2016 (SSI 2016/191)** - Main procedural rules
- **Bankruptcy (Scotland) Act 2016** - Primary legislation for sequestration
- **Insolvency Act 1986** - Primary legislation for corporate insolvency (extended to Scotland)
