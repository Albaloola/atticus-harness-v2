## Draft interlocutor

The Lord Ordinary, having considered the representative party's application and productions, authorises the following group definition for the purposes of these group proceedings:

**Group:** All persons who [objective criteria] during [date range] in relation to [product/service/event/transaction], excluding [clear exclusions].

## Exclusions

Exclude persons who: have already settled/released claims; are time-barred if the court so determines; are directors/officers/related parties if necessary; have conflicting interests; or otherwise fall outside the authorised definition.

## Rationale

Explain common issues, manageability, ascertainability, representative adequacy, remedy fit, and why the definition is neither over- nor under-inclusive.

## Administration

State process for identifying group members, updating register, resolving disputes about membership, notice, data protection, and reporting to court.
