## Key Legislation & Rules, FCA Disclosure Guidance and Transparency Rules (DTR 4)
- UK Market Abuse Regulation (UK MAR)
- IAS 34 (Interim Financial Reporting)
- Companies Act 2006 (accounts and reports)
- UK Corporate Governance Code, AIM Rules for Companies (Rule 18)

## Filing Notes, Half-yearly reports filed via PIP (RNS, PR Newswire, Business Wire) and FCA NSM, Must also be published on company website (DTR 4.2.10R)
- Scottish companies: Companies House Edinburgh for annual accounts, No quarterly filing required; half-yearly filing deadline = 3 months from period end
