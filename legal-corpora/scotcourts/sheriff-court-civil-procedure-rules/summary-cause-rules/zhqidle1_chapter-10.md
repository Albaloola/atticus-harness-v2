# Zhqidle1 Chapter 10

ACT OF SEDERUNT (SUMMARY CAUSE RULES) 2002 NO. 132


CHAPTER 10 	COUNTERCLAIM


10.1	 Counterclaim


Counterclaim
10.1.	(1)	If a pursuer intends to oppose a counterclaim, he must lodge answers within seven days of the lodging of the form of response.
(2)	The pursuer must at the same time as lodging answers intimate a copy of any answers to every other party.
(3)	The defender may apply for warrant for interim diligence in respect of a counterclaim.
