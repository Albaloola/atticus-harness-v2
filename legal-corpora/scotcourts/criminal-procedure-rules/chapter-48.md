# Chapter 48

ACT OF ADJOURNAL (CRIMINAL PROCEDURE RULES) 1996 SI 1996/513

CRIMINAL PROCEDURE RULES 1996
Revoked w.e.f 31 March 2023 subject to saving provision in SSI 61/2023

PART VII  Miscellaneous procedures
CHAPTER 48 PROTECTION OF CHILDREN AND PREVENTION OF SEXUAL OFFENCES (SCOTLAND) ACT 2005

48.1	Interpretation
48.2	Sexual offences prevention orders
48.3	Variation, renewal or discharge of sexual offences prevention orders


Interpretation
48.1	In this Chapter "sexual offences prevention order" means a sexual offences prevention order made where subsection (2) or (3) of section 104 of the Sexual Offences Act 2003 applies.

Sexual offences prevention orders
48.2	A sexual offences prevention order shall be in Form 48.2.

Variation, renewal or discharge of sexual offences prevention orders
48.3	An application for the variation, renewal or discharge of a sexual offences prevention order shall be made by petition in Form 48.3.
