# Scots Legal Forms, Digital Media Law

## Relevant Scottish Court & Tribunal Forms

### Court of Session (Commercial Actions)
- **Form 1.1** - Commercial Action Initial Writ (Chapter 47A Rules of the Court of Session)
- **Form 1.2** - Commercial Action Defences
- **Form 2.1** - IP Sheriff Court procedure (sheriffdom-based IP disputes)

### Summary Sheriff Court (Civil Online)
- **CLAIM FORM (Simple Procedure)** - For lower-value digital media disputes
- **RESPONSE FORM** - Defender's response in Simple Procedure claims

### ICO (Information Commissioner's Office)
- **ICO Complaint Form** - Data protection complaints (UK GDPR / DPA 2018)
- **Subject Access Request Template** - For individual rights requests

### Ofcom (Online Safety)
- **Ofcom Enforcement Referral** - Complaints re OSA 2023 breaches
- **Ofcom Transparency Report Template** - Category 1/2 service obligations

### Copyright (UKIPO)
- **UK Intellectual Property Office** - Copyright infringement notification
- **CDPA s. 97A Application** - Blocking injunction application to Court of Session

### Relevant Court Rules
- **Rules of the Court of Session (RCS)** - Chapter 47A (Commercial Actions), Chapter 55 (Judicial Review)
- **Act of Sederunt (Simple Procedure)** - 2016/228
- **Sheriff Court Rules** - Ordinary Cause Rules, Summary Cause Rules (for lower value claims)
