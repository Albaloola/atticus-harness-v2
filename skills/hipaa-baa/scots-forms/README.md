# HIPAA BAA, Scots/UK Forms

## Key UK Templates

### NHS England Data Processing Agreement Template, Source: https://www.england.nhs.uk/document-store/template-data-processing-agreement/
- Standard DPA template for NHS England, adaptable for Scotland

### Health and Care IG Panel Standardised Agreement, Source: https://www.digitalregulations.innovation.nhs.uk/regulations-and-guidance-for-adopters/all-adopters-guidance/data-protection-agreements-and-contracts/
- Standardised data sharing and processing agreement template

### ICO Resources, Guide to Data Protection: https://ico.org.uk/for-organisations/uk-gdpr-guidance-and-resources/
- Contracts and liabilities under Art 82 UK GDPR: https://ico.org.uk/

### NHS Scotland Specific, Intra-NHS Scotland Information Sharing Accord: https://www.digihealthcare.scot/
- NHS Scotland Information Governance Framework: https://www.informationgovernance.scot.nhs.uk/
- DSP Toolkit: https://www.dsptoolkit.nhs.uk/

### Key Legislation, UK General Data Protection Regulation (retained EU Reg 2016/679)
- Data Protection Act 2018
- National Health Service (Scotland) Act 1978
- Access to Health Records Act 1990
- Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations 2003 (SI 2003/2426)
