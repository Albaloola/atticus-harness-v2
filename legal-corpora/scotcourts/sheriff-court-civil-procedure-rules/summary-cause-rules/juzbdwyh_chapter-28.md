# Juzbdwyh Chapter 28

ACT OF SEDERUNT (SUMMARY CAUSE RULES) 2002 NO. 132


CHAPTER 28 	ACTION OF FURTHCOMING


28.1	 Expenses included in claim


Expenses included in claim
28.1.	The expenses of bringing an action for furthcoming, including the expenses of the arrestment, shall be deemed to be part of the arrestor's claim which may be paid out of the arrested fund or subject.
