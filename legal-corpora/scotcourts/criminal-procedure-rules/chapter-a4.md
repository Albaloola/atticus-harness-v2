# Chapter A4

ACT OF ADJOURNAL (CRIMINAL PROCEDURE RULES) 1996 SI 1996/513
SCHEDULE 2
CRIMINAL PROCEDURE RULES 1996

PART II  General
CHAPTER A4 EXCUSAL OF PROCEDURAL IRREGULARITIES


A4.1	Application to court to excuse procedural irregularity


Application to court to excuse procedural irregularity
A4.1	(1)	An application made in writing under section 300A of the Act of 1995 (power of court to excuse procedural irregularities) shall be in Form A4.1 and shall be served on the other parties to the proceedings.
(2)	On an application referred to in paragraph (1) being made, the court may appoint a diet for a hearing and intimate the diet to the other parties
