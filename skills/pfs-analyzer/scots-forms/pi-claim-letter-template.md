# Personal Injury Claim Letter, Scotland Template

> Verify the applicable Scottish pre-action protocol, time bar, defender identity, insurer route, and forum before sending.

## Parties

Pursuer: [name/DOB/address]. Defender/insurer: [name/address/policy/ref]. Accident: [date/time/location].

## Allegations

Set out duty, breach, mechanism, causation, injury, loss, and supporting evidence.

## Injuries and treatment

Summarise injuries, treatment, prognosis, employment impact, care needs, and medical records/expert evidence required.

## Losses

Attach schedule of loss. Identify wage loss, services, care, travel, treatment, property damage, future loss, interest.

## Documents requested

Accident report, risk assessments, CCTV, photographs, maintenance/inspection records, training records, insurance details, and relevant correspondence.

## Preservation

Require preservation of physical/digital evidence including CCTV, logs, maintenance records, emails, metadata, and accident materials.

## Response deadline

State protocol deadline or proposed reasonable deadline after live verification.
