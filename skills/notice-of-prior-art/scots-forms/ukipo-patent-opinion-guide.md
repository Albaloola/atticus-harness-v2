# UKIPO Patent Opinion Service

The UKIPO provides a non-binding opinion service on patent validity and infringement.

## Key Documents

- **Request form**: Patents Form 2
- **Filing**: UKIPO online portal
- **Rules**: Patents Rules 2007 (SI 2007/3291)
- **Guide**: https://www.gov.uk/guidance/patents-opinions-service

## Relevant Legislation

- Patents Act 1977, ss. 1-3 (patentability, novelty, inventive step)
- Patents Act 1977, s. 72 (revocation grounds)
- European Patent Convention, Arts. 52-56 (harmonised)
