# Scottish Pre-Action Protocol & Demand Letter Checklist

## Key Legislation
- **Courts Reform (Scotland) Act 2014** - court structure, judicial expenses, Simple Procedure
- **Contract (Scotland) Act 1997** - contract validity
- **Prescription and Limitation (Scotland) Act 1973** - time bars
- **Damages (Scotland) Act 2011** - damages for personal injury
- **Interest on Damages (Scotland) Act 1958** - pre-judgment interest
- **Civil Evidence (Scotland) Act 1988** - evidence rules
- **Requirements of Writing (Scotland) Act 1995** - formal writing

## Court Tracks, Scotland
| Track | Value | Court | Expenses |
|---|---|---|---|
| Simple Procedure | £0-£5,000 | Sheriff Court | Fixed (£150-£250 per stage) |
| Ordinary Cause | £5,000+ | Sheriff Court | Party-party taxation |
| Personal Injury | Any | Sheriff PI Court (£5k-£100k) or Court of Session (£100k+) | PI expenses scale |
| Court of Session | £100k+/complex | Outer House | Court of Session scale |

## PI Pre-Action Protocol (Scotland)
1. Letter of Claim → sent to Defender
2. Defender's response within 14 days (extendable to 21-28)
3. Form PI2 (medical authority) served
4. Medical records obtained
5. Schedule of Past and Future Loss prepared
6. Joint Minute of Agreement where possible
7. Pre-proof (pre-trial) meeting

## Sample Demand Structure
> **FORMAL LETTER OF CLAIM, BEFORE ACTION**
>
> Under the Pre-Action Protocol for Personal Injury Claims (Scotland)
>
> We act for [PURSUER]. We refer to [incident/breach] on [date].
>
> **Facts:** [chronological summary referenced to exhibits]
>
> **Legal Basis:** [delict of negligence / breach of contract / statutory duty]
>
> **Damages:** 
> - Solatium (pain and suffering): £[X]
> - Past loss of earnings: £[Y]
> - Future loss of earnings: £[Z]
> - Medical expenses: £[W]
> - **Total: £[TOTAL]**
>
> **Demand:** Our client demands payment of £[TOTAL] within 21 days of this letter. If payment is not received, our client shall raise court proceedings without further notice.
>
> **Judicial Expenses:** Our client seeks full judicial expenses and interest under the Interest on Damages (Scotland) Act 1958.
