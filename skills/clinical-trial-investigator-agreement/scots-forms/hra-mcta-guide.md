# HRA Model Clinical Trial Agreement (mCTA) Guide

The Health Research Authority provides model Clinical Trial Agreements for commercially sponsored trials in the NHS/HSC.

## Key Documents

- **mCTA** (Model Clinical Trial Agreement): template for sponsor-NHS/HSC organisation
- **CRO-mCTA**: for CRO-led studies
- **CI Agreement**: Chief Investigator agreement template
- **Schedule of Events / Statement of Activities**: per HRA standards

## Regulatory Framework

- SI 2004/1031 - Medicines for Human Use (Clinical Trials) Regulations 2004
- SI 2025/538 - Clinical Trials (Amendment) Regulations 2025
- UK GDPR / Data Protection Act 2018
- ICH GCP E6(R2/R3)
- HRA Approval process, NHS Research Scotland (NRS) for Scottish sites, Adults with Incapacity (Scotland) Act 2000
