# Scottish/UK Franchise Resources

## Industry Body

- **British Franchise Association (bfa):** [https://www.thebfa.org](https://www.thebfa.org)
  - Code of Ethics for self-regulation (UK has no statutory franchise disclosure regime equivalent to the US FTC Franchise Rule)

## Key UK Legislation

- **Competition Act 1998** - governs anti-competitive agreements; block exemption for vertical agreements applies to franchise arrangements
- **Vertical Agreements Block Exemption Regulation** - EU-derived framework retained post-Brexit; relevant to resale price maintenance and territorial restrictions
- **Consumer Rights Act 2015** - may apply to B2C aspects of franchise operations
- **Data Protection Act 2018** - implements UK GDPR; governs processing of franchisee and customer personal data

## Key Scottish Legislation

- **Contract (Scotland) Act 1997** - distinctive Scots contract law framework; no requirement for consideration
- **Requirements of Writing (Scotland) Act 1995** - governs formal validity of documents; execution requires witnessing (not notarisation)

## Precedents & Further Reading

- **Practical Law UK** - full precedent franchise agreements and renewal clauses for UK/Scottish practice
