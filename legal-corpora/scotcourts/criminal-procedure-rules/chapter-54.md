# Chapter 54

ACT OF ADJOURNAL (CRIMINAL PROCEDURE RULES) 1996 SI 1996/513
SCHEDULE 2
CRIMINAL PROCEDURE RULES 1996


PART VII  Miscellaneous procedures
CHAPTER 54 MUTUAL RECOGNITION OF CRIMINAL FINANCIAL PENALTIES


54.1	Form of certificate

Form of certificate
54.1. 	A certificate issued under section 223A(1) of the Act of 1995 (recognition of financial penalties: requests to other member States) shall be in Form 54.1.
