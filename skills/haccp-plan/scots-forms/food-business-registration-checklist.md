# Food Business Registration Checklist, Scotland

> Verify current local authority requirements and Food Standards Scotland guidance before use.

## Registration data

- Food business operator legal name, trading name, company number/charity number if applicable.
- Premises address, correspondence address, contact details.
- Activity type: catering, retail, manufacturing, mobile, online, import/export, storage/distribution.
- Start date and operating hours.
- Water supply, waste arrangements, pest control, cleaning schedule.
- Food Safety Management System/HACCP documentation.
- Allergen management, labelling, traceability, recall procedure.
- Staff training and supervision records.

## Evidence pack

Premises plan, process flow, menu/product list, supplier list, temperature logs, cleaning rota, allergen matrix, training log, recall mock test, calibration log.

## Submission log

Record local authority, submission date, acknowledgement, inspection date, officer contact, conditions, remedial actions, and deadlines.
