# Environmental Health Officer Audit Preparation, Scotland

## Pre-audit checklist

- Registration acknowledgement and any prior inspection report.
- HACCP/Food Safety Management System current version.
- Temperature records, cleaning records, pest control, training records.
- Allergen matrix and customer information process.
- Supplier traceability and recall procedure.
- Maintenance/calibration records.
- Corrective action log and management review.

## During audit

Keep a note of questions, samples, documents reviewed, verbal concerns, deadlines, and requested follow-up.

## After audit

Create action plan with owner, deadline, evidence of completion, and source/guidance checked.
