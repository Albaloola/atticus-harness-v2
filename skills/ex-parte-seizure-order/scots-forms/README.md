# Scots Forms Reference, Ex Parte Seizure Order (Without Notice Interim Diligence)

## Court Forms

| Form | Description | Source |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Form G9 | Interim diligence application (Sheriff Court Ordinary Cause) | Ordinary Cause Rules 1993, Chapter 4 |
| Form 13.3 | Inhibition application (Sheriff Court) | Ordinary Cause Rules, Form 13.3 |
| Form of Minute | Without notice application (Court of Session) | Rules of the Court of Session 1994, Ch. 13 |
| Schedule of Arrestment | Statutory form for intimation to arrestee | Bankruptcy and Diligence etc. (Scotland) Act 2007, Sch. 2 |
| Form of Interlocutor | Granting interim diligence (standard style) | Court of Session / Sheriff Court practice |

## Key Legislation

- Bankruptcy and Diligence etc. (Scotland) Act 2007 (asp 3)
- Court of Session Act 1988, s.47 (interim interdict)
- Rules of the Court of Session 1994 (SSI 1994/290)
- Ordinary Cause Rules 1993 (SI 1993/1956)
- Sheriff Courts (Scotland) Act 1907
