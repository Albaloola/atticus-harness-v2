# UK Corporate Compliance Framework, Guidance Note

## Governing Frameworks (UK equivalents to DOJ ECCP / FSG Ch. 8)

| US Framework | UK Equivalent |
|---|---|
| DOJ Evaluation of Corporate Compliance Programs (ECCP) | UK Corporate Governance Code (FRC) / FCA Handbook (SYSC, PRIN) |
| Federal Sentencing Guidelines Chapter 8 | No direct equivalent, UK Sentencing Council Guidelines for Corporate Offenders |
| SEC enforcement priorities | FCA regulatory priorities (Consumer Duty, Market Abuse, Financial Crime) |
| Caremark duties (Delaware) | Directors' duties under Companies Act 2006, s.172 (duty to promote success) |

## Key UK Regulatory Bodies

- **FCA** (Financial Conduct Authority) - market conduct, consumer protection, financial crime
- **PRA** (Prudential Regulation Authority) - financial stability
- **FRC** (Financial Reporting Council) - corporate governance, audit, actuarial
- **CMA** (Competition & Markets Authority) - competition/antitrust
- **ICO** (Information Commissioner's Office) - data protection
- **HSE** (Health & Safety Executive) - workplace safety
- **SEPA** (Scottish Environment Protection Agency) - environmental regulation in Scotland

## Core UK Compliance Statutes

- Bribery Act 2010 (s.7: corporate failure to prevent bribery)
- Companies Act 2006 (directors' duties, s.172 to 177)
- Financial Services and Markets Act 2000 (FSMA)
- UK GDPR / Data Protection Act 2018
- Equality Act 2010
- Consumer Rights Act 2015
- Competition Act 1998 / Enterprise Act 2002
- Modern Slavery Act 2015 (s.54: transparency in supply chains)
- Criminal Finances Act 2017 (failure to prevent tax evasion)
- Corporate Manslaughter and Corporate Homicide Act 2007

## Key Differences from US

- **UK Corporate Governance Code**: "comply or explain" rather than prescriptive rules
- **Bribery Act 2010 s.7**: strict liability for failure to prevent bribery, only defence is "adequate procedures"
- **No Sarbanes-Oxley Act**: UK equivalents through Companies Act + FCA Disclosure Guidance
- **Whistleblowing**: PIDA 1998 protects workers; no SEC bounty equivalent
- **Records retention**: 6 years typical (Companies Act); HMRC requires 5+ years
- **Employment**: ACAS Code of Practice; Employment Tribunal jurisdiction

## Scotland-Specific Points

- Scottish Sentencing Guidelines apply in Scottish courts, COPFS prosecutes corporate crime in Scotland (not CPS)
- SEPA replaces Environment Agency in Scotland, Property/environmental regulation devolved to Scottish Parliament, Personal injury limitation: 3 years (Prescription and Limitation (Scotland) Act 1973)
