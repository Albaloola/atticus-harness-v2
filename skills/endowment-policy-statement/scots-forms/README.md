# Scottish/UK Charity Forms & References

Relevant forms, registers, and references for Scottish charity endowment management.

## OSCR (Scottish Charity Regulator)

| Form / Reference | Purpose | Link |
|---|---|---|
| OSCR Charity Registration Application | Register a new Scottish charity | [OSCR website](https://www.oscr.org.uk/registering/registering-a-charity) |
| OSCR Annual Return | Annual filing, includes endowment fund disclosure | Filed via OSCR Online |
| OSCR Changes Reporting | Notify changes to trustee board, constitutional documents, or structure | [OSCR Online](https://www.oscr.org.uk/registering/changes-to-a-charity) |
| OSCR Guidance on Endowments | Managing restricted and permanent endowment funds | [OSCR Endowments Guidance](https://www.oscr.org.uk/guidance-and-forms/guidance-on-endowments) |
| OSCR Investment of Charity Funds | Guidance on trustee investment duties under 2005 Act | [OSCR Investment Guidance](https://www.oscr.org.uk/guidance-and-forms/investment-of-charity-funds) |
| OSCR Charities SORP Guidance | Accounting and reporting for charity endowments | [OSCR SORP page](https://www.oscr.org.uk/guidance-and-forms/accounts-and-accounting) |
| SCH1 (Application Form) | OSCR registration application form | Available on OSCR website |

## HMRC (Tax)

| Form / Reference | Purpose |
|---|---|
| ChA1 | Application for recognition as a charitable institution for HMRC tax purposes |
| ChV1 | Validation of charitable institution for tax reliefs |
| CT600 | Company Tax Return (for charitable companies and trading subsidiaries) |
| Gift Aid declarations | Claiming tax relief on donations |
| HMRC Charity Tax Reliefs Guidance | Covers trading exemptions, business rates relief, Gift Aid |
| HMRC Trading by Charities Guidance | Covers primary purpose trading, fundraising events, trading subsidiaries |

## UK Accounting & Governance

| Reference | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Charities SORP (FRS 102) | Mandatory accounting framework, governs endowment fund accounting, disclosure, and fund accounting |
| FRC UK Stewardship Code | Relevant for larger charities engaging external investment managers |
| FRC UK Corporate Governance Code | Relevant for larger charitable companies |
| ICAS / CASS Guidance | Professional guidance on charity investment governance |

## Governing Document Templates

| Type | Notes |
|---|---|
| SCIO Constitution (OSCR model) | Model constitution for Scottish Charitable Incorporated Organisations, includes provisions on permanent endowment |
| Trust Deed | Traditional form for charitable trusts with endowment assets |
| Memorandum & Articles (Charitable Company) | For charitable companies limited by guarantee |
| OSCR Model Governing Documents | Available at [OSCR Model Docs](https://www.oscr.org.uk/registering/model-governing-documents) |

## Regulatory Bodies

| Body | Role |
|---|---|
| **OSCR** - Scottish Charity Regulator | Charity registration, regulation, annual returns, guidance on endowment management |
| **HMRC** - HM Revenue & Customs | Charity tax reliefs, Gift Aid, Corporation Tax for trading |
| **FCA** - Financial Conduct Authority | Regulation of investment managers and financial promotions |
| **PRA** - Prudential Regulation Authority | Prudential regulation (where applicable for regulated investment activities) |
| **FRC** - Financial Reporting Council | Accounting standards (FRS 102), Stewardship Code |
| **ICAS** - Institute of Chartered Accountants of Scotland | Professional body; charity governance guidance |

## Key Legislation

| Statute | Relevance |
|---|---|
| Charities and Trustee Investment (Scotland) Act 2005 | Part 6, ss.42 to 49: charity investment powers; ss.66 to 69: trustee duties; permanent endowment provisions |
| Trusts and Succession (Scotland) Act 2024 | Recent reforms affecting trustee duties and endowment management |
| Charities SORP (FRS 102) | Accounting and reporting framework for UK charities |
| Trustee Act 2000 | Applies in England/Wales only; not directly applicable in Scotland |
| Corporation Tax Act 2010 | Parts 11, 11A: charitable trading exemptions |
