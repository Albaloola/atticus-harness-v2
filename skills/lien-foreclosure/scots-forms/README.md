# Scotland/UK Forms Reference, Construction Payment Actions

## Key UK Legislation

- Housing Grants, Construction and Regeneration Act 1996 (Part II: Adjudication)
- Scheme for Construction Contracts (Scotland) Regulations 1998 (SSI 1998/64)
- Prescription and Limitation (Scotland) Act 1973
- Contract (Scotland) Act 1997
- Requirements of Writing (Scotland) Act 1995
- Sheriff Court Ordinary Cause Rules 1993
- Court of Session Rules 1994 (Chapter 40 - Ordinary Actions)

| AIA A101 (Stipulated Sum) | SBCC (Scottish Building Contract Committee) Standard Building Contract |
| AIA A102 (Cost Plus) | SBCC Measured Term Contract |
| AIA A201 (General Conditions) | SBCC Sub-Contract |
| AIA A401 (Subcontract) | SBCC Domestic Sub-Contract |
| ConsensusDocs | JCT (Joint Contracts Tribunal) Scotland-specific editions |

## Key Forms

- **Adjudication Notice** - Formal notice of intention to refer a dispute to adjudication
- **Referral Notice** - Full case submission to the adjudicator
- **Adjudicator's Decision** - Binding interim decision enforceable in the Sheriff Court
- **Summary Application** - Sheriff Court form for enforcement of an adjudicator's decision
- **Initial Writ** - For Court of Session construction claims (Ordinary Action)
- **Charge for Payment** - Pre-diligence demand after decree obtained

## Key UK Organisations

- **SBCC (Scottish Building Contract Committee)** - Scottish-specific construction contracts
- **RICS (Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors)** - Adjudicator nominating body
- **CIArb (Chartered Institute of Arbitrators)** - Adjudicator nominating body
- **Scottish Courts and Tribunals Service** - Court procedures
- **Companies House** - Company searches for deeds of registration
- **Registers of Scotland** - Land Register / Sasines for heritable property searches
