# Recall Plan, Scots/UK Forms

The following forms and guidance templates are relevant to UK/Sco­tland product recalls.
Direct download links may not be available as they change with guidance updates; refer to the
source URLs below or search the regulator's website for the latest version.

## Medicinal Products (MHRA)
- **MHRA Defect Report Form** - Available via MHRA website
  - Source: https://www.gov.uk/guidance/defect-reporting-and-recall
  - Recall notification template (Class 1, 2, 3)
- **MHRA Drug Alerts portal:** https://www.gov.uk/drug-device-alerts

## Food (FSA / Food Standards Scotland)
- **FSA/FSS Guidance on Food Traceability, Withdrawals and Recalls (PDF)**
  - Source: https://www.food.gov.uk/business-guidance/food-incidents-product-withdrawals-and-recalls
  - Annex H: Consumer recall notification template
  - Annex K: Key principles template
- **FSS Alerts:** https://www.foodstandards.gov.scot/alerts
- **Food Incident Report Form** - Contact local authority Environmental Health or FSA/FSS Food Incidents

## Medical Devices (MHRA)
- **Field Safety Corrective Action (FSCA) template** - Available via MHRA
  - Source: https://www.gov.uk/guidance/field-safety-corrective-actions
- **Device Alerts portal:** https://www.gov.uk/drug-device-alerts

## General Consumer Products (OPSS)
- **Product Safety Report (PSR) / Recall notification form** - Submit via Product Safety Database
  - Source: https://www.gov.uk/guidance/product-recalls-and-alerts
  - Guidance: https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/notifications-of-unsafe-and-noncompliant-products
  - PAS 7100:2022 Code of Practice (BSI)

## Scotland-Specific
- **IRIC (Incident Reporting & Investigation Centre)** - NHS Scotland medical device incident reporting
  - Source: https://www.nss.nhs.scot/pharmacy-and-medicines-division/incident-reporting-and-investigation-centre/

## UK Legislation, The Human Medicines Regulations 2012 (SI 2012/1916)
- Medical Devices Regulations 2002 (SI 2002/618, as amended)
- Food Safety Act 1990
- General Product Safety Regulations 2005 (SI 2005/1803) / UK GPSR, Health and Safety at Work etc. Act 1974
- Consumer Rights Act 2015 (product liability provisions)
