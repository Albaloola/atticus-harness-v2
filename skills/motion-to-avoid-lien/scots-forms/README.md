# Scottish/UK Forms, Lien/Security Challenge (Insolvency)

## Key Forms & References

| Purpose | Form / Reference | Issuer |
|---------|-----------------|--------|
| Application for reduction | Initial Writ (Ordinary Cause) | Sheriff Court / Court of Session |
| Petition for reduction | Petition (Court of Session) | Court of Session |
| Reduction of unfair preference | s. 98, Bankruptcy (Scotland) Act 2016 | AiB / Court |
| Gratuitous alienation challenge | s. 97, Bankruptcy (Scotland) Act 2016 | AiB / Court |
| Challenge to floating charge | s. 245, Insolvency Act 1986 | Companies Court |
| Extortionate credit challenge | s. 100, Bankruptcy (Scotland) Act 2016 | AiB / Court |
| Sequestration petition | Form 1 (Schedule 1, Bankruptcy (Scotland) Act 2016) | Sheriff Court / AiB |
| Protected Trust Deed | Form PTD1 | AiB |

## Scotland-Specific

- **Accountant in Bankruptcy (AiB)**: https://www.aib.gov.uk, Overseeing body for Scottish sequestration
- **Registers of Scotland**: https://www.ros.gov.uk, For standard securities and land registration
- **Companies House**: https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/companies-house, For registered charges
- **Sheriff Court**: Sequestration and reduction actions (Ordinary Cause)
- **Court of Session**: Complex reduction actions; appeals

## Key Scottish Statutes

| Statute | Key Provisions |
|---------|---------------|
| Bankruptcy (Scotland) Act 2016 | ss. 97 (gratuitous alienations), 98 (unfair preferences), 99 to 100 (extortionate credit, excessive pension contributions), 101 (challenge to floating charges) |
| Insolvency Act 1986 | ss. 238 to 245 (transactions at undervalue, preferences, floating charges), 423 (transactions defrauding creditors) |
| Bankruptcy and Diligence etc. (Scotland) Act 2007 | Reform of diligence; standard securities; abolition of certain heritable securities |
| Conveyancing and Feudal Reform (Scotland) Act 1970 | Standard security regime (Schedule 2, 3, 4) |
| Requirements of Writing (Scotland) Act 1995 | Formal validity of documents |
