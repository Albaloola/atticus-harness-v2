# Chapter 35

ACT OF ADJOURNAL (CRIMINAL PROCEDURE RULES) 1996 SI 1996/513
SCHEDULE 2
CRIMINAL PROCEDURE RULES 1996

PART VII  Miscellaneous procedures
CHAPTER 35 COMPUTER MISUSE ACT 1990

35.1	Notices in relation to relevance of external law


Notices in relation to relevance of external law
35.1	A notice under section 8(5) of the Computer Misuse Act 1990 (notice by defence that conditions not satisfied) shall be served on the prosecutor not later than 14 days before the trial diet.
