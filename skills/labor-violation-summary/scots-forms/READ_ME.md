Scottish/UK forms and resources for Employment Violation Summary skill (adapted from US FLSA/OSHA/NLRA model).

Key forms and links:
- ACAS Early Conciliation form: www.acas.org.uk/early-conciliation, Employment Tribunal ET1 form: www.gov.uk/employment-tribunal/make-a-claim, HSE RIDDOR reporting form: www.hse.gov.uk/riddor, HMRC National Minimum Wage compliance: www.gov.uk/national-minimum-wage

Key resources:
- ACAS Code of Practice on Disciplinary and Grievance Procedures, Equality and Human Rights Commission (EHRC) - code of practice on employment, Employment Tribunal in Scotland: Glasgow, Edinburgh, Dundee, Aberdeen, Employment Appeal Tribunal (EAT) - Edinburgh for Scottish appeals

Key differences from US:
- No at-will employment; unfair dismissal requires 2 years' continuous service, No FLSA liquidated damages; injury to feelings = Vento bands (updated annually)
- Employment Tribunal: 3 months less 1 day time limit (with ACAS early conciliation pausing)
- HSE is sole enforcer for H&S (no private right of action)
- Trade union recognition via CAC, not NLRB
