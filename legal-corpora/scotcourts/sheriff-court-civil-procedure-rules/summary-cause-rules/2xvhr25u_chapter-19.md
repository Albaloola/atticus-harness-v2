# 2xvhr25u Chapter 19

ACT OF SEDERUNT (SUMMARY CAUSE RULES) 2002 NO. 132


CHAPTER 19 	CHALLENGE OF DOCUMENTS


19.1	 Challenge of documents


Challenge of documents
19.1.	(1)	If a party relies on a deed or other document to support his case, any other party may object to the deed or document without having to bring an action of reduction.
(2)	If an objection is made, the sheriff may order the objector, if an action of reduction would otherwise have been competent, to find caution or to consign with the sheriff clerk a sum of money as security.
