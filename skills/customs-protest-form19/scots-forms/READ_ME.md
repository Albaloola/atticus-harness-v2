Scottish/UK forms and resources for Customs Protest skill (adapted from CBP Form 19).

UK forms (no direct equivalent to CBP Form 19):
- C88 (SAD) Single Administrative Document, main customs declaration (now largely electronic via CDS)
- C18 Post-clearance Demand Note, HMRC demand for underpaid duty, C19 Quota Preference Refusal Notice, BTI Decision template, Binding Tariff Information (3-year validity)

Key resources:
- UK Global Tariff (UKGT): www.trade-tariff.service.gov.uk, HMRC tariff classification service: tariff.classification@hmrc.gov.uk, BTI application: www.gov.uk/guidance/apply-for-a-binding-tariff-information-decision, First-tier Tribunal (Tax) forms: www.gov.uk/tax-tribunal, Taxation (Cross-border Trade) Act 2018

Note: Scottish-based appellants appeal to the Court of Session (Inner House), not the E&W Court of Appeal.
