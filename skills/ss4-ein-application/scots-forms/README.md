# Scottish/UK Forms for Business Registration

The following forms are relevant and may be downloaded from GOV.UK:

1. **HMRC Corporation Tax Registration** - Available via HMRC online (Government Gateway). No paper form, registration is done online through the HMRC portal.
2. **Companies House IN01** - Application to register a company. Available from: https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/company-incorporation-and-registration-in01
3. **VAT Registration (VAT1)** - Available from: https://www.gov.uk/government/publications/vat-application-to-register-for-vat-vat1
4. **PAYE Registration** - Done online via HMRC's PAYE online service or through payroll software
5. **Self Assessment Registration** - Available online via HMRC Government Gateway
6. **HMRC CT41G** - Corporation Tax registration form sent to new companies after incorporation; available from HMRC

All forms are also accessible via the HMRC form finder at: https://www.gov.uk/government/collections/hmr&c-forms
