**[FORM, DRAFT]**

IN THE COURT OF SESSION

[Case number]

**PARTY NAME** (Pursuer)
v
**PARTY NAME** (Defender)

**[DATE]**

**INTERLOCUTOR**

The Lord Ordinary [name], having heard [counsel/solicitor for the parties], and having considered the motion for partial summary decree:

1. Finds that the defender has no defence to articles [x] to [y] of the initial writ relating to [specify: e.g. quantum/liability];

2. Accordingly grants partial summary decree in respect of those articles against the defender for [specify: e.g. £[x] / declarator / implement];

3. Reserves the question of expenses;

4. Remits the remaining claims (articles [z] to [w]) for further procedure (proof / procedure roll);

5. Orders [any other directions for case management].

**[Signature of Lord Ordinary / Clerk of Court]**
