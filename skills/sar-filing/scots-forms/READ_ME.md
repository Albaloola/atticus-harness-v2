Scottish/UK forms and resources for SAR Filing skill (adapted from US FinCEN SAR Form 111).

Key resources:
- NCA SAR Portal: www.nationalcrimeagency.gov.uk/what-we-do/crime-threats/money-laundering-and-illicit-finance/suspicious-activity-reports, NCA SAR Guidance Chapter 2: Submitting a SAR (December 2025)
- JMLSG Guidance Notes: www.jmlsg.org.uk, POCA 2002 Part 7 (ss. 327-340)
- Law Society of Scotland AML guidance

Key Scottish context:
- Scottish courts handle POCA confiscation orders and cash seizure, COPFS prosecutes money laundering in Scotland, Law Society of Scotland (not SRA) supervises Scottish solicitors for AML, Scottish limited partnerships require enhanced due diligence

Critical differences from US:
- NO minimum threshold (all suspicions must be reported)
- NO statutory filing deadline (report "as soon as practicable")
- DAML consent process (unique to UK)
- Tipping-off criminal offence under POCA s. 333 (up to 5 years)
- No single SAR form, SAR Portal online
