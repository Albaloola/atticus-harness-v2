# Scotland/UK Forms & References for Due Diligence Summary

## Key UK Transactional Documents

| Document | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| SPA (Share Purchase Agreement) | UK equity purchase agreement |
| APA (Asset Purchase Agreement) | UK business/asset acquisition |
| SHA (Shareholders' Agreement) | Governance for joint ventures |
| Warranty & Indemnity Deed | Seller protections and buyer recourse |
| Disclosure Letter | Qualifies warranties; UK-specific concept |
| Tax Covenant / Deed of Tax Covenant | Tax risk allocation |
| TUPE Letter | Employee transfer under Transfer of Undertakings Regulations |
| CMA Clearance | Competition clearance (Competition & Markets Authority) |
| OFSI Licence | Sanctions clearance (Office of Financial Sanctions Implementation) |

## UK Corporate Forms

| Document | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Annual Confirmation Statement (CS01) | Companies House filing (replaces Annual Return) |
| Register of Persons with Significant Control (PSC) | UK beneficial ownership register |
| Filed Accounts | Micro-entity, small, or full accounts at CH |
| Company Constitution (Articles of Association) | Governance rules |
| Board Minutes / Written Resolutions | Corporate decision documentation |
| Companies Act 2006 Compliance | Directors' duties, transactions with directors |

## Scottish-Specific Transactional Forms

- **Disposition** - transfer of heritable property (Scotland-only deed)
- **Standard Security** - Scottish security over land
- **Search Reports** - Property search (equivalent to US title insurance)
- **Land Register Title Sheet** - Scottish Land Register extract (equivalent to title insurance)
- **Miscellaneous** - personal search, company search, CCJ search

## Key UK Regulatory Replacements

| US Agency | UK Equivalent | Notes |
|-----------|---------------|-------|
| SEC (Securities Exchange Commission) | FCA (Financial Conduct Authority) / PRA | Prospectus regulation, market abuse, listing rules |
| EPA (Environmental Protection Agency) | SEPA (Scotland) / EA (England) / NRW (Wales) | Environmental permitting, contaminated land |
| OSHA | HSE (Health and Safety Executive) | Workplace health & safety |
| OFAC | OFSI (Office of Financial Sanctions Implementation) | Sanctions list screening |

| FTC (Federal Trade Commission) | CMA (Competition & Markets Authority) | Competition, consumer protection |
| DOJ / SEC | SFO (Serious Fraud Office) / FCA | Fraud, bribery investigations |
| FINRA | FCA Conduct Rules | Financial services conduct |
| IRS | HMRC | Tax authority, NOL tracking |

## UK Environmental Due Diligence

- **Phase I / Phase II Desktop Study** - no direct equivalent to ASTM E1527-21; use RICS guidance on environmental due diligence
- **Contaminated Land (Part IIA, EPA 1990)** - liability framework
- **Environmental Permitting Regs (2016)** - NPDES/TITLE V/RCRA equivalents
- **Waste (Scotland) Regulations 2012** - waste management
- **Climate Change (Scotland) Act 2009** - net-zero obligations
- **Environment Act 2021** - biodiversity net gain (England; Scotland has separate provisions)
- **PFAS** - emerging UK regulatory focus (not yet as developed as US)
