# Scottish Construction Defect Resources

## Key Legislation
- **Building (Scotland) Act 2003** - primary building standards legislation for Scotland
- **Building Standards (Scotland) Regulations** - mandatory technical standards replacing IBC
- **Prescription and Limitation (Scotland) Act 1973** - 5-year delictual prescriptive period; 20-year long-stop; 3 years for personal injury
- **Housing Grants, Construction and Regeneration Act 1996** - statutory right to adjudication (Part II provisions apply in Scotland)

## Pre-Action Protocol
- **Pre-Action Protocol for Construction and Engineering Disputes (Scottish version)** - encourages pre-action correspondence and early expert disclosure; failure to comply may affect expenses

## Court Rules
- **Ordinary Cause Rules (OCR)** - Sheriff Court procedure for claims over £5,000
- **Rules of the Court of Session 1994** - Outer House procedure for high-value/complex cases

## Key Cases
- **Donoghue v Stevenson [1932] AC 562** - foundation of delict/negligence duty of care in Scots law
- **Junior Books v Veitchi [1983] SC (HL) 125** - limited exception for pure economic loss in delict where special relationship akin to contract exists
