## Scottish Case Law Considerations

| Case | Relevance |
|---|---|
| *Glasgow City Council v Board of Management of the Clydeport Fund* (2009) | On variation of charitable purposes and cy-près, relevant for permanently restricted endowments where original purposes become impracticable |
| *Scottish Society for the Prevention of Vivisection v OSCR* (2007) | OSCR regulatory oversight; public benefit test, relevant when investment decisions affect charitable purpose |
| *OHRA v OSCR* (2015) | On what constitutes charitable purposes; foundation of trustee decision-making for investments |
| *Ross v Davy* (1996) | Scots trust law on fiduciary duties of trustees, foundational for understanding s.66 to 69 duties |

## OSCR Endowment Categories

| Category | Definition | Reversibility |
|---|---|---|
| **Permanent Endowment** | Donor-restricted, capital must be held indefinitely; income only may be spent | Irreversible except by court order (cy-près) or OSCR scheme |
| **Expendable Endowment** | Donor-restricted, capital may be spent but subject to terms of gift | Per gift terms |
| **Restricted Fund** | Donor-restricted for a particular purpose, includes but not limited to endowment | Per gift terms; may be time-limited |
| **Designated Fund** | Board-designated from unrestricted funds, functionally similar to quasi-endowment | Revocable by Board of Trustees |
| **Unrestricted Funds** | Free for any charitable purpose | Fully under trustee discretion |

## SORP Fund Accounting Classification

Per Charities SORP (FRS 102), endowments must be classified and disclosed in the accounts as one of:

1. **Permanent endowment** - capital held indefinitely
2. **Expendable endowment** - capital expendable under terms of trust
3. **Restricted income funds** - income subject to restrictions (may be endowment or non-endowment)

Notes to the accounts must disclose:
- Nature and purpose of each material endowment fund, Movements in the period, Terms of any restrictions, Any material designated funds created from endowment income

## Key Compliance Dates (Annual Cycle)

| Timeline | Action |
|---|---|
| 9 months after year-end | File annual accounts and return with OSCR |
| Within 3 months of any change | Notify OSCR of trustee changes or constitutional amendments |
| Annually (variable) | Board review of investment policy (recommended good practice) |
| Periodically | OSCR may request information on endowment investment policies; no fixed schedule |
