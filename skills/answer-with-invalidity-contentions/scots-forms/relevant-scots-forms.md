# Scotland/UK Forms & References for Patent Invalidity Defence

## Key UK Patent Court Forms

| Form | Purpose | Link / Notes |
|------|---------|-------------|
| N1(CC) | Claim Form for IPEC | CPR Part 63 PD |
| N1C | Notes for Defendant on responding | CPR Part 63 |
| N9 | Acknowledgment of Service | 14 days (UK) / 21 days (Scotland) |
| Defence & Counterclaim | Combined defence + revocation | CPR Part 16 / Chapter 43A Rules of the Court of Session |
| IPEC Claim Form | Intellectual Property Enterprise Court | Lower costs, capped damages track |

## Scottish Court Forms (Court of Session)

- **Initial Writ** - for patent actions commencing in the Outer House, Court of Session
- **Defences** - response to Initial Writ (similar to Defence under CPR)
- **Counterclaim** - for revocation of patent
- **Note of Argument** - summary of legal submissions

## Key UK Patent Legislation

- **Patents Act 1977** (as amended) - primary UK patent statute
- **Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988** - related IP
- **Patents Rules 2007** (SI 2007/3291) - procedural rules for UKIPO
- **Intellectual Property (Unjustified Threats) Act 2017** - threats actions

## Key CPR & Rules of Court Provisions

- **CPR Part 63** - Intellectual Property claims (England & Wales)
- **PD 63** - Practice Direction for IP claims
- **PD 63 Annex (iPEC)** - IPEC specific procedure (5 day trial cap, 2 day case management)
- **Rules of the Court of Session, Chapter 55** - Intellectual property actions (Scotland)
- **Act of Sederunt (Sheriff Court Intellectual Property) 2021** - Sheriff Court IP jurisdiction
