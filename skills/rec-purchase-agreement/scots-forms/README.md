# Scotland/UK Forms Reference, REGO/ROC Purchase Agreement

## Key UK Energy Certificates (in lieu of US RECs)

| US Certificate | UK Equivalent |
|---|---|
| REC (Renewable Energy Certificate) | REGO (Renewable Energy Guarantee of Origin) - disclosure / corporate reporting |
| REC (Compliance) | ROC (Renewables Obligation Certificate) - compliance with Renewables Obligation |
| WREGIS Certificate | Ofgem REGO Register |
| PJM-GATS Certificate | Ofgem Renewables and CHP Register |
| Green-e Certification | Ofgem REGO scheme / RE100 criteria |

## Key UK Legislation

- Electricity Act 1989
- Utilities Act 2000
- Energy Act 2004, 2008, 2011, 2016
- Renewables Obligation (Scotland) Order (various years, annually updated)
- Renewable Energy Guarantees of Origin (Scotland) Regulations 2022
- Climate Change (Scotland) Act 2009 / net-zero target, Contracts for Difference (CfD) Regulations

## Key UK Regulatory Bodies

- **Ofgem** - Office of Gas and Electricity Markets (Glasgow and London offices)
- **DESNZ** - Department for Energy Security and Net Zero (formerly BEIS)
- **Scottish Government Energy Directorate** - Scottish-specific energy policy
- **SEPA** - Scottish Environment Protection Agency (environmental permits)
- **Low Carbon Contracts Company** - CfD counterparty
- **Electricity Settlements Company** - RO buy-out fund administrator

## Key Market Indices

- **Argus REGO/ROC Prices** - Market pricing data for UK environmental certificates
- **ICIS REGO/ROC Prices** - Independent market assessments
- **Trade Registry** - Voluntary registry platform for UK energy attribute tracking

## Scottish-Specific Considerations

- Renewables Obligation (Scotland) - separate Order from England & Wales; different banding levels, Scottish CfD allocation, often higher proportion of Scottish projects win CfD rounds, Land reform and community benefit, Scottish Government guidance on community benefit from renewable projects, SEPA consents, environmental permits for Scottish generators, Planning, Town and Country Planning (Scotland) Act 1997; National Planning Framework 4
