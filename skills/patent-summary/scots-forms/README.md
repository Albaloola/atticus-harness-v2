# Scotland/UK Forms Reference, Patent Summary

## Key UK IPO Forms (in lieu of USPTO forms)

| US Form | UK Equivalent |
|---|---|
| USPTO Utility Application | UK patent application (Form 1 / Patents Form 1) |
| USPTO Provisional Application | No direct equivalent (UK uses priority date system under Paris Convention) |
| USPTO Continuation | UK divisional application (Patents Form 7) |
| PTO/SB/08 (Decl. & Oath) | Statement of inventorship (Patents Form 7) |
| Information Disclosure Statement (IDS) | No formal IDS obligation (duty of disclosure under UK law is narrower) |
| USPTO Notice of Allowance | UK IPO Intention to Grant (Form 25) |
| USPTO Issue Fee | Grant fee (Patents Form 27) |
| Terminal Disclaimer | No direct equivalent in UK/EPO practice |
| RCE (Request for Continued Exam) | No direct equivalent (compliance period procedure instead) |

## Key UK Legislation

- Patents Act 1977 (as amended)
- Patents Rules 2007 (SI 2007/3291)
- Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988
- European Patent Convention (EPC 2000)

## Key UK/European Bodies

- **UK IPO** - UK Intellectual Property Office, Newport, South Wales
- **EPO** - European Patent Office, Munich (Munich, The Hague, Berlin, Vienna)
- **CIPA** - Chartered Institute of Patent Attorneys
- **IPReg** - Intellectual Property Regulation Board

## UK IPO Patents Manual

- Manual of Patent Practice (MoPP): https://www.gov.uk/guidance/manual-of-patent-practice, EPO Guidelines for Examination: https://www.epo.org/law-practice/legal-texts/guidelines.html

## Scottish Patent Litigation

- Court of Session, Outer House (Commercial Court), Parliament House, Edinburgh, Procedure: Chapter 43 (Commercial Actions) of the Court of Session Rules 1994
