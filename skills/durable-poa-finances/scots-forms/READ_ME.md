Scottish/UK forms and resources for Continuing Power of Attorney skill (adapted from US durable POA).

Key forms:
- OPG Continuing Power of Attorney prescribed form (Schedule 1 to the Adults with Incapacity (Procedure) (Scotland) Regulations 2018)
- OPG Welfare Power of Attorney prescribed form, EPOAR portal (Electronic Power of Attorney Registration): epoar.publicguardian-scotland.gov.uk

Key resources:
- Adults with Incapacity (Scotland) Act 2000
- OPG Scotland: www.publicguardian-scotland.gov.uk, Registration fee: currently £83 (verify current rate)

Critical differences from US:
- Registration with OPG is MANDATORY, POA cannot be used until registered, Certificate provider (solicitor, doctor, or AMHO) must certify capacity, Continuing (financial) and Welfare (personal) are separate legal categories, No US-style "springing" POA, can specify incapacity-only but must be stated, HMRC agent authority is separate from POA
