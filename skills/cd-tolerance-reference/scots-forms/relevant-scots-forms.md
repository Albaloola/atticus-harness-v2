# Scotland/UK Forms & References for Mortgage Disclosure

## Key UK Mortgage Disclosure Documents

| Document | Purpose | Source |
|----------|---------|--------|
| ESIS (European Standardised Information Sheet) | Standard pre-contract mortgage disclosure, fees, APR, repayment | Consumer Credit (Amendment) (EU Exit) Regs 2018 |
| KFI (Key Facts Illustration) | Pre-contract illustration for regulated mortgage contracts | FCA MCOB 5 (historic; now largely superseded by ESIS) |
| Illustration (MCOB 5.4) | Personalised illustration showing total cost, fees, interest rate | FCA Handbook MCOB 5 |
| Offer Document | Formal mortgage offer with binding terms | MCOB 6 |
| Mortgage Conditions | Standard security terms and conditions | Varies by lender |

## Scottish Property Transaction Documents

| Document | Purpose |
|----------|---------|
| Mortgage Offer / Formal Loan Offer | Lender's formal offer of loan secured over heritable property |
| Standard Security | Scottish equivalent of mortgage deed (registered in Land Register) |
| Disposition | Transfer of title to heritable property |
| Offer of Loan/Letter of Loan Offer | Contains binding fee/rate disclosure |
| Home Report (Single Survey + EPC + Property Questionnaire) | Pre-sale pack required in Scotland |
| Missives | Exchange of legally binding offer and acceptance (Scots contract) |

## FCA Regulatory Framework

- **FCA Handbook, MCOB (Mortgage Conduct of Business)** - primary conduct rules
- **CONC (Consumer Credit sourcebook)** - for second-charge lending
- **PERG (Perimeter Guidance)** - regulated activity scope
- **Mortgage Credit Directive Order 2015** (SI 2015/910) - UK implementation
- **Financial Services and Markets Act 2000 (FSMA)** - primary legislation

## Key Differences from US TRID

No direct equivalent to TRID tolerance categories in UK law. Key differences:

- UK does not have a zero/10%/unlimited tolerance system for fee variances, Mortgage offers typically include a binding fee quote; any change requires a new offer, The FCA remedies for mis-stated fees are: complaint → FOS redress; s.75 CCA claims; regulatory enforcement, No 60-day post-closing cure protocol as in US TRID, ESIS must be provided at least 7-14 days before completion, Cancellation rights (14-day cooling-off) for certain mortgages
