# Minute for Arrestment on the Dependence, Scotland Template

## Orders sought

Warrant for arrestment on the dependence against assets/funds held by [arrestee] for [defender].

## Grounds

Set out prima facie case, specific risk of dissipation, proportionality, value of claim, assets targeted, and why lesser protective steps are insufficient.

## Safeguards

Address caution, restriction by amount, service, review, and prejudice to defender/third parties.
