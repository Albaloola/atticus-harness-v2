## Key Legislation

- Rules of the Court of Session 1994, Ch. 37 (recovery of documents)
- Ordinary Cause Rules 1993, Ch. 4 (recovery of documents)
- Data Protection Act 2018
- UK GDPR (retained, with modifications)
- Law Reform (Miscellaneous Provisions) (Scotland) Act 1985 (commission evidence)
