# Ofxdlh2k Chapter 3 Part 04 Actions Lodged Before 30 September 2010

ACT OF SEDERUNT (SUMMARY APPLICATIONS, STATUTORY APPLICATIONS AND APPEALS ETC. RULES) 1999

Statutory Instrument 1999 No. 929 (S. 65)


CHAPTER 3: RULES ON APPLICATIONS UNDER SPECIFIC STATUTES

PART IV: CONVEYANCING AND FEUDAL REFORM (SCOTLAND) ACT 1970
Actions lodged before 30 September 2010

3.4.1	Application
3.4.2	Disposal of applications under Part II of the Act


Application
3.4.1	(1)	In this Part, "the Act" means the Conveyancing and Feudal Reform (Scotland) Act 1970.
(2)	This Part applies to applications and counter applications under Part II of the Act.

Disposal of applications under Part II of the Act
3.4.2	An interlocutor of the sheriff disposing of an application or counter application under Part II of the Act shall be final and not subject to appeal except as to a question of title or as to any other remedy granted.
