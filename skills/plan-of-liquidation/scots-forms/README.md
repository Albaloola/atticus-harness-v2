## Scotland-Specific

- **Edinburgh Gazette**: https://www.edinburgh-gazette.co.uk, Required for notices concerning Scottish companies
- **Registers of Scotland**: https://www.ros.gov.uk, For heritable property transfers
- **Companies House (Scotland)**: https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/companies-house, Scottish companies register
- **Insolvency Service**: https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/insolvency-service
- **Accountant in Bankruptcy**: https://www.aib.gov.uk, For personal insolvency in Scotland

## Key Scottish/UK Statutes

| Statute | Key Provisions |
|---------|---------------|
| Insolvency Act 1986 | Part IV (winding up), ss. 74 to 117 (voluntary winding up), ss. 117 to 162 (compulsory winding up) |
| Insolvency (Scotland) Rules 2016 | Procedural rules specific to Scotland (SSI 2016/397) |
| Companies Act 2006 | Part 31 (dissolution and restoration), s. 1003 (striking off) |
| Company Directors Disqualification Act 1986 | Director disqualification in insolvency |
| Employment Rights Act 1996 | Redundancy rights during insolvency |
| Pensions Act 2004 | Employer debt / PPF |
| Trade Union and Labour Relations (Consolidation) Act 1992 | Collective redundancy consultation (s. 188) |

## Key HMRC References

- CT600 (Corporation Tax Return) - mark as final return, VAT deregistration, VAT7 form, PAYE scheme closure, full final RTI submission and P45s, P11D, final benefits return, HMRC notification of liquidation, via agent or direct to HMRC insolvency team

## Scotland-Specific Practitioner Notes

- Liquidator must be an authorised insolvency practitioner (ICAS, ACCA, IPA, etc.)
- No winding-up petition publication requirement in Edinburgh Gazette until petition presented, Sheriff Court (Scotland) has jurisdiction for compulsory winding up if company's registered office is in Scotland (paid-up share capital ≤ £120,000)
- Court of Session (Outer House) for larger/ complex compulsory windings, Heritable property (land) disposals must comply with Land Registration (Scotland) Act 2012
- Preferential debts: employees (up to £800 per employee), pension contributions, Prescribed part under s. 176A of IA 1986 applies to floating charge realisations
